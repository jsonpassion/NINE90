# Product Return Policy Update

Effective March 1, our return policy has been updated. Customers may now return products within 45 days of purchase, extended from the previous 30-day window. A receipt or proof of purchase is still required for all returns. Damaged items may be exchanged at no additional cost.

## Q124. What changed in the return policy?
- A. Returns are no longer accepted
- B. The return window was extended to 45 days
- C. Receipts are no longer needed
- D. Only exchanges are allowed
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Extended from 30 to 45 days.

## Q125. What is required for a return?
- A. A manager's signature
- B. The original packaging
- C. A receipt or proof of purchase
- D. A written explanation
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Receipt or proof of purchase is required.

## Q126. What happens with damaged items?
- A. They cannot be returned
- B. They may be exchanged at no extra cost
- C. A repair fee is charged
- D. Only store credit is given
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Damaged items may be exchanged at no additional cost.
