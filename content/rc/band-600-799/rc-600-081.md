# Revised Policy on Remote Work Equipment Reimbursement

To: All Department Heads
From: Christine Hale, Vice President of Operations
Date: January 15

Following the successful transition to our hybrid work model last year, the executive committee has approved a revised policy regarding equipment reimbursement for remote work setups. The updated policy, which takes effect on February 1, aims to ensure that all employees working from home have access to the tools and technology necessary to maintain productivity.

Under the new policy, employees who work remotely at least three days per week are eligible for an annual equipment allowance of up to $1,500. This allowance may be used to purchase ergonomic office furniture, monitors, keyboards, headsets, and other peripherals directly related to their job functions. Employees working remotely one to two days per week will receive a reduced allowance of $750.

To claim the reimbursement, employees must submit itemized receipts through the expense management system within 30 days of purchase. All equipment purchased with company funds remains company property and must be returned if the employee leaves the organization. The IT department will maintain a list of approved equipment vendors on the company intranet to help employees make informed purchasing decisions.

Department heads are responsible for verifying their team members' remote work schedules and approving reimbursement requests. A detailed FAQ document addressing common questions about the policy will be circulated next week. In the meantime, please direct any urgent inquiries to the Operations team at operations@company.com.
