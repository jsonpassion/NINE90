# E-Commerce Platform Upgrade and Migration Plan

To: All Online Retail Staff
From: James Whitfield, Director of E-Commerce
Date: July 18

After extensive evaluation, we have selected NextGen Commerce as our new e-commerce platform, replacing the current system that has been in use for the past seven years. The migration will take place over a 10-week period beginning August 15, with the new platform scheduled to go live on October 25.

The decision was driven by several factors, including the need for improved mobile shopping functionality, faster page load times, and better integration with our inventory management and customer relationship management systems. The new platform is expected to reduce page load times by 40 percent, which industry research shows can increase conversion rates by up to 15 percent.

The migration will be conducted in three phases. Phase 1 (August 15 to September 5) will involve the transfer of product catalog data, including descriptions, images, pricing, and inventory levels. Phase 2 (September 6 to October 5) will focus on migrating customer accounts, order histories, and loyalty program data. Phase 3 (October 6 to October 24) will be dedicated to comprehensive testing and staff training on the new system.

During Phase 3, a parallel testing environment will be available for all team members to practice using the new platform. I strongly encourage everyone to spend at least four hours in the testing environment before the go-live date. A series of training webinars will also be offered during October, with schedules to be announced in early September.
