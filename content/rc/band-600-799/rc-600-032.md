# Professional Reference Letter for Regional Manager Candidate

Dear Selection Committee,

I am writing to recommend Jennifer Castillo for the position of Regional Manager at Linden Park Hospitality Group. Ms. Castillo served as Assistant General Manager at our Grandview Conference Hotel for four years, from 2021 to 2025, and I had the privilege of working closely with her as her direct supervisor during that entire period.

Ms. Castillo's most notable achievement was leading the renovation and repositioning of our conference center facilities. When she assumed responsibility for this project, guest satisfaction scores for our meeting and event spaces were at 72 percent, placing us below the regional average. Through her leadership, which included redesigning the booking process, upgrading audiovisual equipment, and implementing a dedicated event coordinator role, satisfaction scores rose to 91 percent within 18 months. Conference revenue increased by 34 percent during the same period.

Beyond operational achievements, Ms. Castillo demonstrated exceptional skill in staff development. She created a structured mentorship program for front-desk associates that reduced first-year turnover from 45 percent to 22 percent. Several employees whom she mentored personally have since been promoted to supervisory positions within our organization.

I should note that Ms. Castillo's departure from our hotel was a significant loss. She relocated to be closer to her family, and I am confident that she would not have left otherwise. Her work ethic, analytical capabilities, and ability to motivate a diverse team are qualities that I have rarely seen combined so effectively in one individual.

I give Ms. Castillo my highest recommendation without any reservation. She would be an outstanding addition to the Linden Park management team, and I am confident she will deliver exceptional results in the Regional Manager role.

Sincerely,
Robert Hwang
General Manager, Grandview Conference Hotel

## Q1. What was the purpose of the conference center renovation project?
- A. To convert the hotel into a residential apartment building
- B. To comply with a government inspection that found safety violations
- C. To improve guest satisfaction and revenue for meeting and event spaces
- D. To prepare for the sale of the property to a new owner
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The project addressed below-average satisfaction scores (72 percent) and successfully raised them to 91 percent while increasing conference revenue by 34 percent.

- A. She was terminated for poor performance.

- D. She was recruited by a competing hotel chain offering a higher salary.

- Explanation: The letter states "She relocated to be closer to her family, and I am confident that she would not have left otherwise."

## Q4. The phrase "without any reservation" in the final paragraph most likely means:
- A. Without booking a hotel room in advance
- B. Without any doubt, hesitation, or qualification

- Explanation: In the context of a recommendation letter, "without any reservation" means the endorsement is given completely and without hesitation.
