# Annual Benefits Enrollment Changes for Next Year

To: All Full-Time Employees
From: Patricia Owens, Director of Human Resources
Date: October 10

The annual benefits enrollment period will open on November 1 and close on November 21. During this time, eligible employees may review and make changes to their health insurance, dental coverage, vision plan, and retirement contribution levels for the upcoming year. Changes made during this period will take effect on January 1.

This year, we are introducing two notable changes to our benefits offerings. First, a new high-deductible health plan option has been added alongside the existing standard and premium plans. The high-deductible plan features lower monthly premiums but a higher annual deductible of $3,000 for individuals and $6,000 for families. Employees who select this plan will be eligible to open a Health Savings Account with a company match of up to $500 annually.

Second, the company's 401(k) retirement plan match has been increased from 4 percent to 5 percent of an employee's salary. This change applies to all employees who have been enrolled in the plan for at least one year. New enrollees will receive the standard 4 percent match during their first year and will automatically be upgraded to the 5 percent match upon their one-year anniversary.

A detailed comparison of all plan options, including estimated costs and coverage summaries, will be mailed to each employee's home address by October 20. Additionally, HR will host informational sessions on November 3, 8, and 15 at 12:00 p.m. in the main auditorium.
