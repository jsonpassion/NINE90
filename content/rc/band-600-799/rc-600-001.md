# Office Relocation Notice

To: All Employees
From: Facilities Management Department
Date: March 3
Subject: Upcoming Office Relocation

We are pleased to announce that Hartwell & Associates will be relocating its headquarters from 240 Commerce Street to the newly constructed Pinnacle Tower at 85 Riverside Boulevard, effective April 15. The decision to relocate was made following a comprehensive review of our space requirements and long-term growth projections.

The new facility offers approximately 40 percent more floor space than our current location, which will allow each department to have dedicated meeting rooms and collaborative work areas. In addition, the building features an on-site fitness center, a cafeteria with subsidized meal options, and underground parking for up to 300 vehicles.

During the transition period from April 10 to April 14, employees will be expected to pack their personal belongings and label all office equipment. Packing supplies will be distributed by the facilities team beginning March 25. Please note that the IT department will handle the transfer of all computer systems and network equipment separately.

Employees who have questions regarding the relocation process are encouraged to attend an informational session scheduled for March 12 at 2:00 P.M. in Conference Room B. Alternatively, inquiries may be directed to Janet Morrison at extension 4502.

## Q131. What is the primary purpose of this notice?
- A. To request feedback on a proposed office layout
- B. To inform employees about a change in office location
- C. To announce the hiring of a new facilities manager
- D. To provide an update on building construction delays
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice announces that the company will be relocating its headquarters to Pinnacle Tower at 85 Riverside Boulevard, effective April 15. The entire message details the relocation plan, timeline, and logistics.

## Q132. What can be inferred about the company's current office?
- A. It lacks sufficient space for the company's needs.
- B. It was recently renovated to improve energy efficiency.
- C. It does not have any parking facilities available.
- D. It is located in a different city than the new office.
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The notice states that the new facility offers "approximately 40 percent more floor space" and that the relocation decision followed a review of "space requirements and long-term growth projections," implying the current office is inadequate in size.

## Q133. What are employees asked to do before April 10?
- A. Submit a relocation preference form to Human Resources
- B. Set up their workstations at the new office location
- C. Attend a mandatory orientation at Pinnacle Tower
- D. Pick up packing supplies distributed by the facilities team
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The notice states that "packing supplies will be distributed by the facilities team beginning March 25," and the transition period starts April 10. Employees need to obtain these supplies before the packing period begins.
