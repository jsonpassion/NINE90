# Internal Memo: Proposed Merger with Calloway Health Systems

To: All Employees
From: Board of Directors, Meridian Medical Technologies
Date: March 5, 2026
Subject: Announcement of Proposed Strategic Merger

The Board of Directors is pleased to announce that Meridian Medical Technologies has entered into a definitive agreement to merge with Calloway Health Systems in a transaction valued at approximately $6.3 billion. This combination will create one of the largest integrated medical technology and healthcare services providers in North America, with combined annual revenues exceeding $11 billion and a workforce of approximately 38,000 employees.

The strategic rationale for this merger is compelling. Meridian's strength in medical device innovation and regulatory expertise complements Calloway's extensive hospital network and digital health platform. Together, the combined entity will be uniquely positioned to offer end-to-end solutions spanning device development, clinical implementation, and patient data management. The board believes this integration will accelerate product development timelines by an estimated 30 percent and unlock approximately $450 million in annual cost synergies within three years.

The transaction is subject to regulatory approval by the Federal Trade Commission and relevant state authorities, a process expected to conclude by Q3 2026. During this review period, both companies will continue to operate independently. Employees should be aware that no immediate changes to organizational structure, compensation, or benefits are anticipated prior to the completion of the merger.

A dedicated Integration Planning Office will be established within thirty days to coordinate the transition. This office will be led jointly by senior executives from both organizations and will provide regular updates through town hall meetings and a dedicated internal communications portal. Employees with questions or concerns are encouraged to contact the Human Resources department or submit inquiries through the confidential feedback channel.

The board is confident that this merger represents a transformative opportunity for both organizations and for the patients and healthcare providers we serve.
