# Quarterly Sales Report

MEMORANDUM

To: Senior Management Team
From: Diana Cho, Director of Sales
Date: January 15
Re: Fourth Quarter Sales Performance Summary

I am writing to present the sales performance results for the fourth quarter of the fiscal year. Overall revenue for Q4 reached $8.7 million, representing a 12 percent increase compared to the same period last year. This growth was driven primarily by strong performance in our enterprise software division, which accounted for 58 percent of total quarterly revenue.

The consumer electronics segment, however, experienced a modest decline of 3 percent, largely due to increased competition from lower-priced alternatives in the domestic market. To address this trend, the product development team has proposed launching a mid-range product line in Q2 of the upcoming fiscal year, which is projected to capture a broader customer base.

Regionally, the Asia-Pacific market showed the most significant improvement, with sales rising by 22 percent year-over-year. Our recently established partnerships with distributors in Vietnam and Indonesia contributed substantially to this growth. Conversely, European sales remained flat, and the team is currently evaluating strategies to revitalize demand in that region.

Looking ahead, I recommend that we increase our marketing budget allocation for the Asia-Pacific region by 15 percent and accelerate the timeline for the mid-range product launch. A detailed proposal outlining these recommendations will be circulated prior to the February board meeting.

- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo presents Q4 sales performance data across divisions and regions, then concludes with recommendations for future strategy, making it a summary-and-recommendation document.
