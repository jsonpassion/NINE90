# International Trade Show Participation Plan

To: Marketing and Sales Teams
From: Nicole Yang, Director of Business Development
Date: April 3

I am pleased to confirm that our company will be exhibiting at the Asia-Pacific Trade Expo in Singapore from June 12 to June 15. This is the region's largest trade event, attracting over 8,000 attendees from 40 countries, and it represents a significant opportunity to showcase our latest products and establish new partnerships.

Our exhibition booth has been reserved in Hall B, which is dedicated to technology and innovation exhibitors. The booth measures 6 meters by 4 meters and will feature interactive product demonstrations, a meeting area for scheduled appointments with potential clients, and a digital presentation wall highlighting our company's capabilities.

I am requesting that each team nominate two representatives to attend the expo. Marketing representatives will be responsible for managing the booth, distributing promotional materials, and collecting visitor contact information. Sales representatives will focus on conducting meetings with pre-qualified leads and following up on existing prospect relationships. All attendees should be prepared to deliver a five-minute product overview in English.

Travel and accommodation arrangements will be coordinated by the administrative team. Please submit your nominated attendees to me by April 15 so that visa applications, flight bookings, and hotel reservations can be processed in a timely manner. A preparatory meeting for all confirmed attendees will be scheduled for the last week of May.
