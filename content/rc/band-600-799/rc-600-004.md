# New Branch Opening

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

CRESTLINE FINANCIAL GROUP ANNOUNCES OPENING OF NEW BRANCH IN DOWNTOWN PORTLAND

Portland, OR — March 5 — Crestline Financial Group is pleased to announce the grand opening of its newest branch location at 1200 Morrison Street in downtown Portland, scheduled for April 1. This marks the company's fifteenth branch in the Pacific Northwest and its third in the greater Portland metropolitan area.

The new 4,500-square-foot facility will offer a full range of personal and commercial banking services, including savings and checking accounts, mortgage lending, small business loans, and investment advisory services. To celebrate the opening, Crestline will offer a promotional interest rate of 4.2 percent on twelve-month certificates of deposit for accounts opened during the first two weeks of operation.

"We have seen tremendous growth in the Portland market over the past several years, and opening this branch allows us to serve our expanding customer base more effectively," said Thomas Nguyen, Regional Vice President. "The Morrison Street location was chosen specifically for its accessibility to both residential neighborhoods and the central business district."

The branch will employ approximately 25 staff members, including relationship managers, loan officers, and financial advisors. Crestline has also partnered with a local nonprofit organization, the Portland Community Development Fund, and will donate $50,000 to support affordable housing initiatives in the area.

An invitation-only reception for local business leaders and community partners will be held on March 28, followed by the public grand opening on April 1. Regular business hours will be Monday through Friday, 9:00 A.M. to 5:00 P.M., and Saturday, 9:00 A.M. to 1:00 P.M.

For more information, visit www.crestlinefinancial.com or contact the corporate communications office at (503) 555-0184.
