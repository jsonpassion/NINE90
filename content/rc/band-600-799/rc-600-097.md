# Employee Mentorship Program Launch

To: All Employees
From: Diana Ross, Learning and Development Manager
Date: February 5

We are excited to announce the launch of the Horizon Mentorship Program, a structured six-month initiative designed to support professional growth through one-on-one mentoring relationships. The program will begin on March 1 and is open to employees at all levels who are interested in either serving as mentors or being matched with a mentor.

Mentors should have at least five years of professional experience, a track record of strong performance, and a genuine interest in developing others. Mentees may be employees at any stage of their career, from recent hires seeking guidance on navigating the company culture to mid-level professionals looking to develop leadership skills. Each mentoring pair will meet for a minimum of two hours per month.

The matching process will be handled by the Learning and Development team based on career goals, skill areas, and departmental preferences indicated in the application. We aim to match participants across different departments to encourage broader organizational understanding and cross-functional networking.

Program participants will attend a kickoff orientation on March 1 and a mid-program review session in June. At the conclusion of the program in September, mentees will present a brief summary of their development experience to their department. Interested employees should submit their applications through the HR portal by February 20.
