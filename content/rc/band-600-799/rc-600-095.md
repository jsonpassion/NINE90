# Commercial Lease Dispute Resolution Notice

From: Park & Williams Law Firm
To: Mr. Gerald Hoffman, Hoffman Trading Co.
Date: November 15
Re: Lease Dispute — 400 Commerce Boulevard, Suite 305

Dear Mr. Hoffman,

We are writing on behalf of our client, Meridian Properties LLC, regarding the outstanding lease dispute for the commercial space located at 400 Commerce Boulevard, Suite 305. Despite several prior communications, the matter of unpaid rent totaling $24,000, covering the months of August through November, remains unresolved.

As outlined in Section 8.2 of the lease agreement signed on January 15 of last year, tenants who fail to pay rent within 30 days of the due date are subject to a late penalty of 5 percent per month on the outstanding balance. Additionally, Section 12.4 provides the landlord with the right to initiate eviction proceedings after 90 days of non-payment.

Our client has expressed a preference for resolving this matter through mediation rather than litigation, as a mutually agreeable outcome would be beneficial for both parties. We propose scheduling a mediation session during the first week of December at the offices of Park & Williams. If an agreement is not reached through mediation, our client reserves the right to pursue legal action, including recovery of unpaid rent, late penalties, and legal fees.

Please respond to this letter within 10 business days to confirm your willingness to participate in mediation. You may contact our office at 555-0712 or email disputes@parkwilliams.com.
