# Formal Partnership Agreement Proposal

March 8, 2026

Mr. Daniel Kwon
Chief Executive Officer
Pinnacle Data Solutions
Seoul, South Korea

Dear Mr. Kwon,

On behalf of Westbrook Analytics International, I am writing to formally propose a strategic partnership between our two organizations. Following extensive discussions at the Global Technology Summit in Singapore and subsequent meetings between our respective business development teams, we believe a collaboration would create significant mutual value in the Asia-Pacific enterprise analytics market.

The proposed partnership would combine Westbrook's proprietary machine learning platform with Pinnacle's established client relationships and deep regional expertise. Under the terms we envision, Westbrook would license its analytics engine to Pinnacle on a preferential basis, while Pinnacle would serve as the exclusive distribution and implementation partner across South Korea, Japan, and Taiwan for an initial period of three years.

Revenue generated through joint engagements would be shared on a 60-40 basis, with the larger share allocated to the party responsible for securing each individual contract. Both companies would contribute equally to a joint marketing fund of $2 million annually to support product awareness campaigns and client acquisition events throughout the designated territories.

To ensure effective governance, we propose establishing a Joint Steering Committee comprising three senior representatives from each organization. This committee would meet quarterly to review performance metrics, resolve operational issues, and evaluate opportunities for expanding the partnership into additional markets. Either party may terminate the agreement with twelve months' written notice, subject to the completion of all outstanding contractual obligations.

We have enclosed a detailed term sheet for your review and would welcome the opportunity to discuss these terms at your earliest convenience. Our legal counsel is prepared to begin drafting the formal agreement upon your preliminary acceptance.

Sincerely,
Margaret Hensley
President, Westbrook Analytics International
