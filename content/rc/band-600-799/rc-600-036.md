# Letter Regarding Service Agreement Renewal Terms

Dear Ms. Langford,

I am writing in reference to the upcoming renewal of our facilities maintenance service agreement, which is scheduled to expire on December 31, 2025. We have valued our partnership with Langford Building Services over the past three years and wish to continue the relationship under terms that reflect the current scope of our operations.

Since the agreement was originally executed, our organization has undergone significant changes. We have expanded from four office locations to seven, adding facilities in Portland, Charlotte, and Austin. Our total managed floor space has increased from 280,000 square feet to 445,000 square feet. Despite this growth, the current agreement covers only the original four locations, and we have been engaging separate local contractors for the three newer sites.

We would like to propose consolidating all seven locations under a single service agreement with Langford Building Services. We believe this approach would improve service consistency, simplify our vendor management processes, and create operational efficiencies for both parties.

To support this proposal, we request the following terms for the renewed agreement: a three-year contract duration with an option to extend for two additional years; inclusion of all seven facilities under a comprehensive service scope covering janitorial, landscaping, and mechanical maintenance; a volume-based pricing structure that reflects the 59 percent increase in total floor space; and quarterly performance reviews with agreed-upon service level benchmarks.

We recognize that expanding the service territory to include three new cities will require Langford to invest in additional staffing and subcontractor relationships. We are prepared to discuss transition timelines and phased implementation if a simultaneous launch across all locations is not feasible.

I look forward to discussing these terms at your earliest convenience.

Best regards,
Thomas Adler
Director of Corporate Real Estate, Beacon Technologies
