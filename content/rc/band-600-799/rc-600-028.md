# Letter of Intent for Strategic Distribution Partnership

Dear Mr. Okamoto,

On behalf of Bridgeport International Trading, I am writing to formalize our interest in establishing a strategic distribution partnership with Sakura Precision Instruments for the North American market. Following our productive discussions at the Tokyo Industrial Equipment Expo in September, we believe there is a strong mutual benefit in combining your product excellence with our extensive distribution network.

Bridgeport currently maintains distribution relationships with over 800 industrial and scientific equipment retailers across the United States, Canada, and Mexico. Our logistics infrastructure includes six regional warehouses strategically located to enable next-day delivery to 94 percent of the continental United States. We also employ a dedicated technical sales team of 45 specialists who are trained to provide product demonstrations and after-sales support.

We propose an exclusive distribution agreement for the North American market covering Sakura's full range of laboratory measurement instruments and industrial calibration equipment. Under the proposed terms, Bridgeport would commit to minimum annual purchase volumes of $12 million in the first year, increasing to $18 million by year three. In return, we would request exclusive distribution rights and a wholesale discount structure of 35 percent below the manufacturer's suggested retail price.

To support the partnership launch, we are prepared to invest $1.5 million in marketing and promotional activities during the first year, including trade show exhibitions, digital advertising campaigns, and a series of regional product demonstration events. We would also establish a dedicated Sakura product support team within our organization.

We understand that you are currently evaluating multiple distribution proposals for the North American market. We are confident that Bridgeport's established market presence, logistics capabilities, and technical expertise position us as the ideal partner. I would welcome the opportunity to present a detailed business plan at your convenience.

Respectfully,
Caroline Whitfield
Senior Vice President, Business Development
Bridgeport International Trading
