# Office Closure Notice

Dear employees,

Please be informed that the main office on the 3rd floor will be closed on Friday, April 10. The cleaning team will work on the carpets and windows during the day. All employees on the 3rd floor should use the meeting rooms on the 2nd floor for that day. Your computers and personal items will be safe. Please do not leave any food or drinks on your desks. The office will reopen on Monday, April 13 at 8:00 A.M. Thank you for your understanding.

— Building Management

## Q1. Why will the office be closed on Friday?
- A. There is a holiday.
- B. The cleaning team will work.
- C. The building is being sold.
- D. There is a meeting.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states that "The cleaning team will work on the carpets and windows during the day."

## Q2. Where should 3rd floor employees work on Friday?
- A. At home
- B. On the 2nd floor
- C. On the 1st floor
- D. In the parking lot
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says employees "should use the meeting rooms on the 2nd floor for that day."

## Q3. When will the office reopen?
- A. Saturday, April 11
- B. Sunday, April 12
- C. Monday, April 13
- D. Tuesday, April 14
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice clearly states "The office will reopen on Monday, April 13 at 8:00 A.M."
