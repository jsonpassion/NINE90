# Safety Training Reminder

To: Factory Floor Staff
From: Safety Manager
Date: May 8

All factory workers must attend the annual safety training session. This year's training will be held on May 20 in the main conference hall.

There are two sessions to choose from:
- Morning session: 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.
- Afternoon session: 1:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Both sessions cover the same material. Please sign up for one session by May 15. You can sign up on the notice board near the break room or by emailing safety@company.com.

Attendance is required. Workers who do not attend will not be allowed to work on the factory floor until they complete the training.

## Q1. How many training sessions are offered?
- A. One
- B. Four
- C. Three
- D. Two
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The memo lists two sessions: a morning session and an afternoon session.

## Q2. What happens if a worker does not attend?
- A. They receive a warning letter
- B. They pay a fine
- C. They cannot work on the factory floor
- D. They must attend next year
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo says workers who do not attend "will not be allowed to work on the factory floor until they complete the training."

## Q3. How can workers sign up for a session?
- A. By calling the safety manager
- B. On the notice board or by email
- C. By visiting the HR office
- D. Through the company website
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo says to "sign up on the notice board near the break room or by emailing safety@company.com."
