# Parking Garage Notice

Maple Street Parking Garage — Rate Change Notice

Effective April 1, the Maple Street Parking Garage will update its parking rates. The hourly rate will increase from $3.00 to $3.50. The daily maximum will change from $18.00 to $20.00. Monthly passes are now available for $150 per month, which saves daily parkers about $280 per year. Monthly pass holders can enter and exit the garage at any time with their access card. All current monthly pass holders will keep their current rate of $130 until June 30. New monthly pass applications are available at the garage office on Level 1. The garage office is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

## Q1. What will the new hourly rate be?
- A. $3.00
- B. $3.50
- C. $4.00
- D. $4.50
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states 'The hourly rate will increase from $3.00 to $3.50.'

## Q2. How much will a new monthly pass cost?
- A. $100
- B. $120
- C. $130
- D. $150
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The notice says 'Monthly passes are now available for $150 per month.'

## Q3. When will current monthly pass holders start paying the new rate?
- A. April 1
- B. May 1
- C. July 1
- D. Immediately
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Current pass holders 'will keep their current rate of $130 until June 30,' so the new rate starts July 1.
