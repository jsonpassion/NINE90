# Dog Park Rules

WESTSIDE DOG PARK — RULES

Open daily from sunrise to sunset. The park is free for all residents.

Rules:
1. All dogs must have current vaccinations and wear a collar with ID tags.
2. Owners must stay in the park while their dog is present.
3. Aggressive dogs must be removed immediately.
4. Pick up after your dog. Waste bags are provided at each entrance.
5. Children under 10 must be accompanied by an adult.
6. No food or glass containers in the off-leash area.
7. The small dog area (under 25 pounds) is on the left side.
8. The large dog area is on the right side.
9. Dogs in heat are not permitted in the park.
10. Maximum of 3 dogs per handler.

## Q1. How many dogs can one person bring?
- A. 1
- B. 2
- C. 3
- D. 4
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Rule 10 states a maximum of 3 dogs per handler.

## Q2. What area is for small dogs?
- A. The right side
- B. The center area
- C. The left side
- D. The back section
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Rule 7 says the small dog area (under 25 pounds) is on the left side.

## Q3. What is NOT allowed in the off-leash area?
- A. Water bottles
- B. Dog toys
- C. Leashes
- D. Food or glass containers
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Rule 6 says no food or glass containers in the off-leash area.
