# Password Change Required

Dear User,

For security reasons, all employees must change their computer password by April 30. Your new password must be at least eight characters long and include one number and one special character.

To change your password:
1. Press Ctrl + Alt + Delete.
2. Click "Change Password."
3. Enter your old password, then your new password twice.
4. Click "OK."

Do not share your password with anyone. Do not write it on paper near your desk. If you forget your password, the IT help desk can reset it for you. Call extension 4400 during business hours.

## Q1. When must employees change their password?
- A. By March 30
- B. By April 30
- C. By May 30
- D. Immediately
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email says employees "must change their computer password by April 30."

## Q2. What must the new password include?
- A. A number and a special character
- B. At least 12 characters
- C. Only letters
- D. The employee's name
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The email says the password "must be at least eight characters long and include one number and one special character."

## Q3. What should employees do if they forget their password?
- A. Create a new email account
- B. Ask a coworker for help
- C. Call the IT help desk at extension 4400
- D. Send an email to the manager
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The email says "the IT help desk can reset it for you. Call extension 4400."
