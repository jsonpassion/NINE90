# Sunny Side Cafe

Welcome to Sunny Side Cafe! We serve breakfast and lunch every day.

Breakfast: 7:00 A.M. – 11:00 A.M.
Lunch: 11:30 A.M. – 3:00 P.M.

Our most popular items: Pancake Set ($8), Egg Sandwich ($6), Chicken Salad ($10), and Soup of the Day ($7). All lunch orders come with a free drink. We are closed on Sundays. Free Wi-Fi is available for all customers. For large group reservations of 8 or more people, please call us at 555-0234 at least one day in advance.

## Q1. What time does breakfast end?
- A. 10:00 A.M.

- D. 3:00 P.M.

- Explanation: The sign shows breakfast hours as "7:00 A.M. – 11:00 A.M."
