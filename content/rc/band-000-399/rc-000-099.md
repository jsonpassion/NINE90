# Weekend Island Tour

Explore beautiful Pine Island with our weekend tour package!

Package includes:
- Round-trip ferry tickets
- Two nights at the Island View Hotel
- Breakfast each morning
- A guided island tour on Saturday

Price: $350 per person (double room)
Single room supplement: $80

Tours run every weekend from June through September. Book at least two weeks in advance to guarantee your spot. Groups of four or more get a 10 percent discount.

To book, call Pine Island Tours at 555-0421 or visit www.pineislandtours.com.
