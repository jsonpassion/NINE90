# Volunteer Opportunity

Volunteers Needed — Greenfield Community Garden

The Greenfield Community Garden is looking for volunteers for our spring planting event on Saturday, April 5, from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We will be planting vegetables and flowers in the new garden beds behind the community center. No gardening experience is needed — we will teach you everything! All tools and gloves will be provided. Please wear comfortable clothes and bring a water bottle. Volunteers who work the full four hours will receive a free lunch. Children under 12 must be with a parent or guardian. To sign up, send an email to garden@greenfield.org or call 555-0423 by April 3.

## Q1. How long is the volunteer event?
- A. 2 hours
- B. 3 hours
- C. 4 hours
- D. 5 hours
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The event runs from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM, which is 4 hours.

- Answer: C

- A. April 1

- D. April 7
- Answer: B
