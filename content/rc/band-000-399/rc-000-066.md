# Restaurant Grand Opening

Grand Opening — Blue Ocean Seafood Restaurant

Blue Ocean Seafood is proud to announce its grand opening on April 3 at 78 Harbor Road. We serve fresh fish, shrimp, lobster, and many other seafood dishes. Lunch specials are available every weekday from 11:30 AM to 2:00 PM, starting at $12. Reservations for dinner are recommended, especially on weekends.
