# City Bus Schedule Change

Starting September 1, the Route 7 bus will have a new schedule. Buses will run every 15 minutes during morning rush hour (7:00 a.m. to 9:00 a.m.) and every 15 minutes during evening rush hour (5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.).

During other hours, buses will come every 30 minutes. On weekends and holidays, buses will run every 45 minutes.

The bus fare stays the same: $2.00 for adults and $1.00 for students and seniors. Monthly passes are available at the main bus station for $60.

For the full schedule, visit www.citybus.com or pick up a timetable at any bus stop.

## Q1. How often will the bus come during morning rush hour?
- A. Every 10 minutes
- B. Every 15 minutes
- C. Every 30 minutes
- D. Every 45 minutes
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says buses run "every 15 minutes during morning rush hour."

## Q2. How much is the bus fare for students?
- A. $0.50
- B. $2.00
- C. $1.50
- D. $1.00
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The notice states the fare is "$1.00 for students and seniors."

## Q3. When do the schedule changes begin?
- A. Immediately
- B. September 1
- C. Next Monday
- D. January 1
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says "Starting September 1, the Route 7 bus will have a new schedule."
