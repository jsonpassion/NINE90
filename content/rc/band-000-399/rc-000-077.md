# Lost and Found

Lost and Found — Westfield Shopping Center

Attention shoppers! The following items were found in the Westfield Shopping Center during the past week and are being held at the Customer Service desk on Level 1, near the south entrance.

- One pair of black reading glasses (found March 17, Food Court)
- One child's blue jacket, size 6 (found March 18, Play Area)
- One silver keychain with three keys (found March 19, Parking Level B2)
- One brown leather wallet containing a bus pass (found March 20, Restroom near Store 14)

Items will be kept for 30 days. After that, unclaimed items will be donated to charity. To claim your item, please bring a photo ID to the Customer Service desk. The desk is open daily from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

## Q1. Where is the Customer Service desk?
- A. On Level 2, near the north entrance
- B. On Level 1, near the south entrance
- C. In the Food Court
- D. At Parking Level B2
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states items are 'held at the Customer Service desk on Level 1, near the south entrance.'

## Q2. What is needed to claim a lost item?
- A. A receipt
- B. A description of the item
- C. A photo ID
- D. A police report

- Explanation: The notice says 'please bring a photo ID to the Customer Service desk.'

## Q3. What happens to unclaimed items after 30 days?
- A. They are thrown away
- B. They are sold
- C. They are donated to charity
- D. They are sent to the police
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states 'unclaimed items will be donated to charity.'
