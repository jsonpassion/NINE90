# Parking Garage Rules

DOWNTOWN PARKING GARAGE — RULES

Hours: Open 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM daily.
Rates: First hour free. Each additional hour is $3. Maximum daily rate is $15.
Monthly pass: $120 per month. Apply at the office on Level 1.
Speed limit: 5 mph on all levels.
Do not park in spaces marked "Reserved" without a permit.
Cars left after 11:00 PM will be charged a $25 overnight fee.
Lost ticket fee: $20.
Report any problems to the attendant at the entrance.

## Q1. How much does parking cost after the first hour?
- A. $1 per hour
- B. $2 per hour
- C. $5 per hour
- D. $3 per hour
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The sign states each additional hour after the first free hour is $3.

## Q2. What is the speed limit in the garage?
- A. 3 mph
- B. 10 mph

- D. 15 mph
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The sign says the speed limit is 5 mph on all levels.

## Q3. What happens to cars left after 11:00 PM?
- A. They will be towed
- B. They will receive a warning
- C. They will be locked in
- D. They will be charged a $25 overnight fee
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The sign states cars left after 11:00 PM will be charged a $25 overnight fee.
