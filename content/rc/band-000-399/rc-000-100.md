# Expense Report Reminder

To: All Employees
From: Accounting Department
Date: November 20

This is a reminder that all expense reports for the current quarter must be submitted by December 15. Please use the new online form available on the company intranet.

Each expense report must include:
- The date of the expense
- A clear description of the business purpose
- A receipt for every item over $25

Reports submitted without proper receipts will be returned. Reimbursement checks will be mailed within 10 business days after approval.

If you need help with the online form, please contact Sarah Kim in accounting at extension 2150.

## Q1. What is the deadline for expense reports?
- A. November 20
- B. November 30
- C. December 31
- D. December 15
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The memo says reports "must be submitted by December 15."

## Q2. When are receipts required?
- A. For all expenses
- B. For items over $25
- C. For items over $50
- D. Only for travel expenses
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo states employees need "a receipt for every item over $25."

## Q3. How long does reimbursement take after approval?
- A. 5 business days
- B. 10 business days
- C. 15 business days
- D. 30 business days
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo says "Reimbursement checks will be mailed within 10 business days after approval."
