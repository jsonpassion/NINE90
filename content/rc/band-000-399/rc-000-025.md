# Employee Dress Code Reminder

To: All Staff
From: HR Department
Date: March 22
Subject: Dress Code Reminder

This is a reminder about our company dress code. On Monday through Thursday, all employees must wear business attire. This means dress shirts, slacks, or skirts. Jeans and sneakers are not allowed on these days. On Fridays, you may wear casual clothes, but shorts and flip-flops are still not permitted. Employees who meet clients must always wear business attire, even on Fridays. If you have questions, please contact Maria Lopez in HR at extension 2240.

## Q1. When can employees wear casual clothes?
- A. Friday
- B. Wednesday
- C. Monday
- D. Every day
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The memo says on Fridays employees may wear casual clothes.

## Q2. What is NOT allowed even on casual Fridays?
- A. Jeans
- B. Shorts
- C. Sneakers
- D. T-shirts
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo states shorts and flip-flops are still not permitted on Fridays.

## Q3. Who should employees contact with questions?
- A. The department manager
- B. The front desk
- C. Maria Lopez in HR
- D. The CEO's office
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo says to contact Maria Lopez in HR at extension 2240.
