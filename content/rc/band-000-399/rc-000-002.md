# Office Supply Order

To: All Staff
From: Administration Office
Date: March 8

The Administration Office will place the monthly office supply order on Friday, March 14. If you need any supplies, please fill out a Supply Request Form and submit it to the Administration Office by Wednesday, March 12.

Supply Request Forms are available at the front desk on the 1st floor or can be downloaded from the company intranet. Each department is allowed a maximum budget of $150 per month for supplies. Orders that exceed this amount must be approved by a department manager.

Common items such as paper, pens, and folders are always kept in the supply room on the 2nd floor. Please check the supply room before submitting a request.

## Q101. When is the deadline for supply requests?
- A. March 8
- B. March 10
- C. March 12
- D. March 14
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states that requests must be submitted by Wednesday, March 12.

## Q102. What happens if an order exceeds $150?
- A. The order will be canceled.
- B. The employee must pay the difference.
- C. It must be approved by a department manager.
- D. The Administration Office will reduce the order.
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says "Orders that exceed this amount must be approved by a department manager."

## Q103. Where can employees find common supplies?
- A. At the front desk on the 1st floor
- B. In the supply room on the 2nd floor
- C. In the Administration Office
- D. On the company intranet
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states that common items are kept in the supply room on the 2nd floor.
