# Office Supply Order

To: All Staff
From: Office Manager
Date: April 5

We will place an office supply order next Monday, April 10. If you need any supplies, please fill out the order form on the shared drive by Friday, April 7. The form asks for your name, department, and the items you need.

Common items such as pens, paper, and folders are always in stock in the supply room on the second floor. The order is only for special items not found in the supply room. Orders usually arrive within five business days.

Please contact Tom Baker at extension 1120 if you need help with the form.

## Q1. What is the deadline for submitting the order form?
- A. April 7
- B. April 5
- C. April 10
- D. April 15
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The notice states that employees should fill out the form "by Friday, April 7."

## Q2. Where can staff find common supplies?
- A. In the main lobby
- B. On the shared drive
- C. In the supply room on the second floor
- D. At Tom Baker's office
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says "Common items such as pens, paper, and folders are always in stock in the supply room on the second floor."

## Q3. Who should employees contact for help?
- A. The office manager
- B. The department head
- C. Tom Baker
- D. The supply room clerk
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says to contact Tom Baker at extension 1120 for help with the form.
