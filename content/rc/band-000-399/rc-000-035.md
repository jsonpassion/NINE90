# Office Supply Ordering

To: All Department Heads
From: Purchasing Department
Date: March 20
Subject: New Office Supply Ordering System

We are changing how office supplies are ordered. Starting April 1, all supply requests must be submitted through the company intranet. Go to "Tools" then "Supply Request." You can order paper, pens, folders, and other basic items. Orders placed before 12:00 PM on Monday will be delivered by Wednesday. Orders after Monday noon will arrive the following week. Each department has a monthly budget of $200 for supplies. Requests over $50 for a single item need manager approval. Contact Tom Reed at extension 3315 if you need help with the new system.

## Q1. Where should supply requests be submitted?
- A. By email to the purchasing department
- B. On a paper form
- C. Through the company intranet
- D. At the supply closet
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo says all supply requests must be submitted through the company intranet.

## Q2. When will Monday morning orders be delivered?
- A. Wednesday
- B. Tuesday
- C. Monday afternoon
- D. Friday
- Answer: A
- Explanation: Orders placed before 12:00 PM on Monday will be delivered by Wednesday.

## Q3. What requires manager approval?
- A. Any order over $20
- B. Orders of more than 10 items
- C. Requests over $50 for a single item
- D. All first-time orders
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo states requests over $50 for a single item need manager approval.
