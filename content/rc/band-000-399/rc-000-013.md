# Now Hiring: Front Desk Assistant

Star Hotel is looking for a front desk assistant. This is a full-time position, Monday through Friday, from 8:00 A.M. to 4:00 P.M. The pay is $15 per hour. You must be friendly, organized, and good with computers. Experience in a hotel is nice but not required. We will train you. You must speak English. Speaking a second language is a plus. To apply, send your resume to jobs@starhotel.com by May 20. Interviews will begin on May 25.
