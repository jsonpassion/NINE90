# Neighborhood Yard Sale

MAPLE GROVE NEIGHBORHOOD YARD SALE

Date: Saturday, April 12
Time: 8:00 AM - 2:00 PM
Location: Maple Grove Community Park

Join your neighbors for our annual spring yard sale! Bring items you no longer need and find great deals. Tables are available for $10 each. You may also sell from the back of your car for free. Food and drinks will be sold by the Maple Grove PTA. All money from food sales goes to Lincoln Elementary School. No pets allowed in the selling area. Free parking at the church lot on Elm Street. Rain date: Saturday, April 19. To reserve a table, call Janet at 555-0387 before April 8.

## Q1. How much does a table cost?

- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says tables are available for $10 each.
