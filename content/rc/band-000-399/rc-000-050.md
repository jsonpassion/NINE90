# Holiday Office Closure

To: All Staff
From: Management
Date: March 22
Subject: Easter Holiday Schedule

The office will be closed on Friday, April 18 (Good Friday) and Monday, April 21 (Easter Monday). Regular business hours resume on Tuesday, April 22. Employees do not need to use vacation days for these holidays.

Important reminders:
- Please complete all urgent tasks before Thursday, April 17 at 5:00 PM.
- Set your email auto-reply before you leave on Thursday.
- The IT help desk will have limited support on April 18 from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM for emergencies only.
- Payroll will be processed one day early. Paychecks will be available on Thursday, April 17 instead of Friday, April 18.

Enjoy the long weekend!

## Q1. How many days will the office be closed?
- A. One day
- B. Two days
- C. Three days
- D. Four days
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The office is closed Friday April 18 and Monday April 21 — two days.

## Q2. When will regular business hours resume?
- A. Monday, April 21
- B. Thursday, April 24
- C. Wednesday, April 23
- D. Tuesday, April 22
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The memo says regular business hours resume on Tuesday, April 22.

## Q3. When will paychecks be available?
- A. Wednesday, April 16
- B. Thursday, April 17
- C. Friday, April 18
- D. Monday, April 21
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Paychecks will be available on Thursday, April 17 instead of the usual Friday.
