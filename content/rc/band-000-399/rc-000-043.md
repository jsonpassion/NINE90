# Laundromat Rules

SPARKLE CLEAN LAUNDROMAT — RULES AND PRICES

Hours: 6:00 AM - 10:00 PM daily. Last wash starts at 9:00 PM.

Washers:
- Small (1 load): $3.00
- Large (2 loads): $5.00

Dryers: $0.25 per 8 minutes. Most loads dry in 32-40 minutes.

Rules:
- Remove clothes within 15 minutes of cycle ending.
- Do not overload machines.
- Clean the lint trap after using dryers.
- No dyeing clothes in the machines.
- Management is not responsible for lost items.

Detergent and fabric softener are available from the vending machine for $1.50 each. An attendant is on duty from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM for assistance. Change machine available near the entrance.

## Q1. What time does the last wash start?

- C. 9:30 PM
- D. 10:00 PM
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The sign says last wash starts at 9:00 PM.
