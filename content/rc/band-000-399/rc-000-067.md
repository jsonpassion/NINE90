# Office Supply Order Form

Office Supply Order Reminder — Henderson & Associates

To: All Staff
From: Office Manager
Date: March 22

The monthly office supply order will be placed on March 28. If you need any supplies, please fill out a request form and leave it in the box at the front desk by March 27 at 5:00 PM. Request forms are available next to the printer on the second floor. Common items like pens, paper, and sticky notes are ordered automatically. You only need to submit a form for special items such as binders, file folders, or presentation materials. Please include your department name and the quantity needed. Orders usually arrive within 5 business days. Contact Lisa at extension 402 if you have questions.

## Q1. When is the deadline to submit a request form?
- A. March 22
- B. March 27 at 5:00 PM
- C. March 28
- D. March 30
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo says to leave forms 'by March 27 at 5:00 PM.'

## Q2. Which items do NOT require a request form?
- A. Binders
- B. File folders
- C. Pens and paper
- D. Presentation materials
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo states 'Common items like pens, paper, and sticky notes are ordered automatically.'

## Q3. How long does delivery usually take?
- A. 2 business days
- B. 3 business days
- C. 5 business days
- D. 7 business days
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo says 'Orders usually arrive within 5 business days.'
