# Parking Lot Rules

Attention All Tenants:

Please read the updated parking lot rules for Maple Towers Apartments.

Each apartment gets one parking space. If you have two cars, you may rent a second space for $50 per month. Visitor parking is available in Lot B near the main entrance. Visitors may park for up to four hours.

Cars without a valid parking sticker will be towed. You can get a parking sticker from the management office on the first floor. The office is open Monday through Friday, 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.

Please do not park in spaces marked "Reserved" or "Fire Lane." Thank you for your cooperation.

## Q1. How much does a second parking space cost?
- A. $25 per month
- B. $100 per month
- C. $75 per month
- D. $50 per month
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The notice says "you may rent a second space for $50 per month."

## Q2. Where should visitors park?
- A. In any open space
- B. In reserved spaces
- C. In Lot B near the main entrance
- D. On the street
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states "Visitor parking is available in Lot B near the main entrance."

## Q3. What happens to cars without a parking sticker?
- A. They receive a warning
- B. They are towed
- C. They get a fine
- D. Nothing happens
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says "Cars without a valid parking sticker will be towed."
