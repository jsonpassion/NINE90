# Neighborhood Watch

Neighborhood Watch Meeting — Oakwood Estates

There will be a neighborhood watch meeting on Thursday, April 10, at 7:00 PM in the Oakwood Community Hall. Officer James Burton from the local police department will speak about home safety tips. Topics will include locking doors and windows, using outdoor lights, and what to do if you see something suspicious. The meeting will last about one hour. Light refreshments will be served. All residents of Oakwood Estates are welcome. If you cannot attend, a summary of the meeting will be posted on the community bulletin board and emailed to all residents by April 14. Please RSVP to the homeowners' association at oakwood@hoa.org by April 8.
