# Farmer's Market Schedule

GREENVILLE FARMER'S MARKET — NOW OPEN FOR SPRING!

The Greenville Farmer's Market returns every Saturday starting April 5.

Location: Town Square, next to City Hall
Hours: 8:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Find fresh fruits, vegetables, baked goods, honey, flowers, and handmade crafts from local vendors. Live music starts at 10:00 AM each week. Children's activities are available in the kids' corner, including face painting and a petting zoo.

Vendor spaces are still available for $35 per Saturday or $120 for the full season (April through October). The market runs rain or shine. Dogs are welcome but must be on a leash. For vendor applications, contact the Parks Department at 555-0721.
