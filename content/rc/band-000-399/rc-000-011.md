# Work Schedule Change

To: All Sales Team Members
From: Manager, David Park
Date: April 2
Subject: New Work Hours

Starting April 15, the sales team will have new work hours. Instead of 9:00 A.M. to 5:00 P.M., the new hours will be 8:30 A.M. to 4:30 P.M. This change is because most of our customers call in the morning. The lunch break will stay the same — 30 minutes from 12:00 P.M. to 12:30 P.M. Please update your calendars. If you have any concerns, talk to me before April 10.

## Q1. What is the new start time?
- A. 8:00 A.M.
- B. 8:30 A.M.
- C. 9:00 A.M.
- D. 9:30 A.M.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo states the new hours are "8:30 A.M. to 4:30 P.M."

## Q2. Why are the hours changing?
- A. The office is moving.
- B. Employees asked for a change.
- C. Most customers call in the morning.
- D. The manager has a new schedule.
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo explains "This change is because most of our customers call in the morning."

## Q3. When should employees share their concerns?
- A. Before April 2
- B. Before April 10
- C. Before April 15
- D. After April 15
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo says "If you have any concerns, talk to me before April 10."
