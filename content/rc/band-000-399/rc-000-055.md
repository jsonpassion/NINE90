# Company Wellness Program

To: All Employees
From: Human Resources
Date: March 20
Subject: New Wellness Program

We are excited to announce our new Employee Wellness Program starting May 1. The program includes free gym membership at FitLife Center, a healthy lunch option in the cafeteria on Wednesdays, and monthly wellness workshops. Topics for workshops include stress management, healthy eating, and sleep improvement. Employees who participate in at least 8 activities by December 31 will receive 2 extra vacation days next year. You can sign up for the program on the HR portal. There is no cost to employees — the company covers everything. A wellness survey will be sent next week to help us plan activities. Please complete it by April 4.
