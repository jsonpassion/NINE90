# Pharmacy Notice

Notice to Customers — Wellness Pharmacy

Wellness Pharmacy will have new hours starting April 1. We will open at 8:00 AM instead of 9:00 AM on weekdays. Weekend hours will stay the same: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Our new pharmacist, Dr. Amy Lee, will be available for free health consultations every Wednesday from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM. No appointment is needed. We also now offer a prescription delivery service for customers within 5 miles of the store. Delivery is free for orders over $30. For smaller orders, a $5 delivery fee applies. To arrange delivery, call us at 555-0284 or use our new app, available on both iOS and Android.

## Q1. What is the new weekday opening time?
- A. 7:00 AM
- B. 8:00 AM
- C. 9:00 AM
- D. 10:00 AM
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states the pharmacy 'will open at 8:00 AM instead of 9:00 AM on weekdays.'

## Q2. When are free health consultations offered?
- A. Monday mornings
- B. Tuesday afternoons
- C. Wednesday from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM
- D. Friday from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says Dr. Lee 'will be available for free health consultations every Wednesday from 2:00 PM to 4:00 PM.'

## Q3. How much is delivery for an order of $25?
- A. Free
- B. $3
- C. $5
- D. $10
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Delivery is free for orders over $30. For smaller orders like $25, 'a $5 delivery fee applies.'
