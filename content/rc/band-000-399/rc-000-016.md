# Community Fun Run Registration

Join the 5th Annual Community Fun Run!

Date: Saturday, June 10
Time: 9:00 A.M.
Location: Central Park
Distance: 3 kilometers

Registration is $10 per person. Children under 12 are free. All runners will receive a free T-shirt and a bottle of water. Register online at www.communityfunrun.com or in person at the Community Center before June 5. Walk-in registration on the day of the event costs $15. Prizes will be given to the top 3 finishers. For questions, email run@community.org.

## Q1. How much does early registration cost?
- A. Free
- B. $5
- C. $10
- D. $15
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The form states "Registration is $10 per person."

## Q3. How much more does walk-in registration cost compared to early registration?
- A. $3 more
- B. $5 more
- C. $10 more
- D. $15 more
- Answer: B
