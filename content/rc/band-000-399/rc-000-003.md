# Parking Policy Update

To: All Employees
From: Facilities Management
Date: March 5

Please be informed that the employee parking policy will change starting April 1. Due to construction work on Parking Lot A, all employees must use Parking Lot B, located behind the main building, until further notice.

Parking Lot B has 200 spaces. Employees are encouraged to arrive early, as spaces are available on a first-come, first-served basis. A free shuttle bus will run every 10 minutes between Parking Lot B and the main entrance from 7:30 a.m. to 9:30 a.m. and from 5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.

Employees who use public transportation will receive a monthly transit pass at a 50% discount. To request a pass, please visit the Facilities Management office on the 1st floor.

## Q101. Why is the parking policy changing?
- A. The company is reducing parking spaces.
- B. Construction work is being done on Parking Lot A.
- C. Parking Lot B is being closed.
- D. New employees need more parking spaces.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states the change is "due to construction work on Parking Lot A."

## Q102. How often does the shuttle bus run?
- A. Every 5 minutes
- B. Every 10 minutes
- C. Every 15 minutes
- D. Every 30 minutes
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says "A free shuttle bus will run every 10 minutes between Parking Lot B and the main entrance."

## Q103. What benefit is offered to employees who use public transportation?
- A. A free parking space
- B. A monthly transit pass at a 50% discount
- C. A company car
- D. Free shuttle bus service all day
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states that employees using public transportation will receive a monthly transit pass at a 50% discount.
