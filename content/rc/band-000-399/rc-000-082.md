# Spring Sale at Greenway Market

Greenway Market is having a big spring sale! From March 20 to March 30, all fruits and vegetables are 30 percent off. Fresh bread and dairy products are 15 percent off.

The store is open every day from 8:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. On the last day of the sale, March 30, the store will close early at 6:00 p.m. Free samples of new products will be available near the entrance every Saturday.

Visit us at 150 Oak Street or check our website at www.greenwaymarket.com for more details. We hope to see you soon!

## Q1. How long does the spring sale last?
- A. One week
- B. Ten days
- C. Two weeks
- D. Eleven days
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The sale runs from March 20 to March 30, which is eleven days.

## Q2. What time does the store close on March 30?
- A. 6:00 p.m.
- B. 8:00 a.m.
- C. 9:00 p.m.
- D. 7:00 p.m.
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The notice states that on March 30, "the store will close early at 6:00 p.m."

## Q3. When can customers get free samples?
- A. Every day during the sale
- B. Every Saturday
- C. Only on March 30
- D. On weekdays only
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says "Free samples of new products will be available near the entrance every Saturday."
