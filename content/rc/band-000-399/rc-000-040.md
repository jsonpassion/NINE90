# Fire Drill Announcement

To: All Employees
From: Safety Committee
Date: March 21
Subject: Fire Drill — April 2

A fire drill will take place on Wednesday, April 2, between 10:00 AM and 10:30 AM. When you hear the alarm, please leave the building through the nearest exit. Do not use the elevators. Walk quickly but do not run. Go to the parking lot on the east side of the building. Each floor has an assigned area. Floor 1: Section A. Floor 2: Section B. Floor 3: Section C. Your floor captain will take attendance. The drill should last about 15 minutes. Please do not return to the building until the all-clear announcement. New employees should review the evacuation map posted near the stairs.

## Q1. When is the fire drill?
- A. April 1
- B. April 5
- C. April 3
- D. April 2
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The memo says the fire drill is on Wednesday, April 2.

## Q2. Where should employees gather?

## Q3. What should employees NOT do during the drill?
- A. Leave through the nearest exit
- B. Use the elevators

- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo says do not use the elevators during the drill.
