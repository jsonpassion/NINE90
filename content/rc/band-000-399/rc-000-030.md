# Company Cafeteria Changes

To: All Employees
From: Facilities Department
Date: March 19
Subject: Cafeteria Renovation

The employee cafeteria on the 2nd floor will be closed for renovation from April 1 to April 14. During this time, a temporary food service will be set up in Conference Room D on the 1st floor. The temporary area will offer sandwiches, salads, and drinks only. Hot meals will not be available during the renovation. A food truck will also be parked outside the main entrance from 11:30 AM to 1:30 PM on weekdays. The renovated cafeteria will have more seating, a new salad bar, and longer hours.

## Q1. How long will the cafeteria be closed?
- A. One week
- B. Three weeks
- C. Two weeks
- D. One month
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo says the cafeteria will be closed from April 1 to April 14, which is two weeks.

## Q2. Where will temporary food service be located?
- A. In Conference Room D on the 1st floor
- B. On the 3rd floor
- C. In the lobby
- D. In the parking garage
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The memo states temporary food service will be in Conference Room D on the 1st floor.

## Q3. What will the food truck hours be?
- A. 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM
- B. 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM
- C. 11:30 AM to 1:30 PM
- D. 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo says the food truck will be available from 11:30 AM to 1:30 PM on weekdays.
