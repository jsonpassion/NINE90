# Library Notice

Greenville Public Library — Important Notice

The Greenville Public Library will be closed from April 5 to April 12 for building repairs. During this time, the book drop box outside the main entrance will remain open. You can return books there at any time. All books due during the closure will have their due dates extended to April 19. No late fees will be charged for these items. The online library catalog will still be available. You can renew books and place holds online at www.greenvillelibrary.org. The children's reading program on April 8 has been moved to the Community Center on Oak Street. Please call 555-0192 for questions.

## Q1. What is the reason for the library closure?
- A. Staff training
- B. A holiday
- C. Building repairs
- D. A special event
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states the library will be closed 'for building repairs.'

## Q2. Where can books be returned during the closure?
- A. At the Community Center
- B. At the book drop box outside the main entrance
- C. At the front desk
- D. At any other library branch
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says 'the book drop box outside the main entrance will remain open.'

## Q3. What happened to the children's reading program on April 8?
- A. It was canceled
- B. It was postponed to April 19
- C. It was moved to the Community Center on Oak Street
- D. It will be held online
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states the program 'has been moved to the Community Center on Oak Street.'
