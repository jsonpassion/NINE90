# New Employee Parking Policy

To: All Staff
From: Office Manager, David Chen
Date: March 21
Subject: Updated Parking Policy

Beginning April 1, employee parking will change. The main lot (Lot A) will be for visitors and clients only. Employees should now park in Lot B behind the warehouse. Lot B has been repaved and has new lighting. A shuttle bus will run between Lot B and the main entrance every 10 minutes from 7:30 AM to 9:00 AM and from 4:30 PM to 6:00 PM. At other times, the walk takes about 5 minutes. Employees with mobility issues may apply for a Lot A permit through HR. Carpoolers who bring 3 or more people will get reserved spots closest to the shuttle stop.

## Q1. Where should employees park starting April 1?
- A. Lot A
- B. Lot B
- C. The street
- D. The visitor garage
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo says employees should now park in Lot B behind the warehouse.

## Q2. How often does the shuttle run during morning hours?
- A. Every 10 minutes
- B. Every 5 minutes
- C. Every 15 minutes
- D. Every 20 minutes
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The shuttle runs every 10 minutes during the specified hours.

## Q3. Who can get reserved spots near the shuttle stop?
- A. Managers
- B. Carpoolers with 3 or more people
- C. New employees
- D. Employees who arrive early
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Carpoolers who bring 3 or more people get reserved spots closest to the shuttle stop.
