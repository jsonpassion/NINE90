# New Employee Welcome

To: All New Employees
From: Human Resources Department
Date: March 10

Welcome to Greenfield Industries! We are happy to have you on our team. All new employees must attend an orientation session on their first day. The session will be held in Conference Room B on the 3rd floor from 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.

During the orientation, you will receive your employee ID badge, learn about company policies, and meet your department supervisor. Please bring a photo ID and your signed employment contract. Lunch will be provided in the cafeteria at 12:30 p.m.

If you have any questions, please contact Ms. Karen Lee in Human Resources at extension 2240.

## Q101. What is the purpose of this notice?
- A. To announce a company holiday
- B. To invite new employees to an orientation session
- C. To request a meeting with managers
- D. To introduce a new department
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice tells new employees they must attend an orientation session on their first day.

## Q102. What should new employees bring?
- A. A laptop and notebook
- B. A photo ID and signed employment contract
- C. Their employee ID badge
- D. A copy of the company policy
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states "Please bring a photo ID and your signed employment contract."

## Q103. Who should new employees contact with questions?
- A. Their department supervisor
- B. The cafeteria manager
- C. Ms. Karen Lee in Human Resources
- D. The conference room receptionist
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says to contact Ms. Karen Lee in Human Resources at extension 2240.
