# Library Hours Update

GREENFIELD PUBLIC LIBRARY — UPDATED HOURS

Starting March 25, our library hours will change. We will open at 9:00 AM instead of 8:00 AM on weekdays. Saturday hours stay the same: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. The library will now also be open on Sundays from 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM. The children's reading room will close 30 minutes before the main library. Please return all books to the front desk or the outdoor drop box. Late fees are 50 cents per day.

## Q1. What time will the library open on weekdays?
- A. 8:00 AM
- B. 12:00 PM
- C. 10:00 AM
- D. 9:00 AM
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The notice states the library will open at 9:00 AM instead of 8:00 AM on weekdays.

## Q2. When will the library be open on Sundays?
- A. 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM
- B. 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM
- C. 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM
- D. 1:00 PM to 5:00 PM
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says the library will be open on Sundays from 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM.

## Q3. How much is the late fee per day?
- A. 50 cents
- B. 25 cents
- C. 75 cents
- D. 1 dollar
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The notice states late fees are 50 cents per day.
