# Computer System Update

To: All Employees
From: IT Department
Date: June 12

The IT Department will update all office computers this weekend. The update will take place on Saturday, June 17, from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.

During the update, you will not be able to use your office computer or email. Please save all your files and close all programs before you leave work on Friday, June 16.

After the update, your computer will start faster and have new security features. If you have any problems on Monday, please call the IT help desk at extension 3300.

## Q1. When will the update happen?
- A. June 12
- B. June 16
- C. June 19
- D. June 17
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The notice says "The update will take place on Saturday, June 17."

## Q2. What should employees do before leaving on Friday?
- A. Turn off their computers
- B. Save files and close programs
- C. Call the IT help desk
- D. Install the update themselves
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice tells employees to "save all your files and close all programs before you leave work on Friday."

## Q3. What will improve after the update?
- A. The internet speed
- B. The email system
- C. Computer startup speed and security
- D. The office Wi-Fi
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states "your computer will start faster and have new security features."
