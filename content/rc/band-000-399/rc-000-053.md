# Concert Venue Information

RIVERSIDE AMPHITHEATER — CONCERT INFORMATION

Gates open: 1 hour before show time
No re-entry after leaving the venue.

Allowed items: Small bags (no larger than 12"x12"), blankets, sealed water bottles
Prohibited items: Outside food, alcohol, umbrellas, chairs, professional cameras

Seating: General admission is first-come, first-served on the lawn. Reserved seats are in rows A-R. Wheelchair-accessible seating is in Section H — please show your ticket at the main gate.

Parking: $15 per car. Lot opens 2 hours before show time. No overnight parking.
Food and drinks are available at concession stands inside the venue.
Lost and found is located at the main gate office.
Emergency exits are marked with green signs throughout the venue.
