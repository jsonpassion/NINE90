# Store Closing Sale

Grand Closing Sale — Parker's Home Goods

Parker's Home Goods on 5th Avenue is closing after 30 years of business. Everything in the store must go! From March 20 to March 31, all items are 40 percent off. Starting April 1, prices will be reduced to 60 percent off. Kitchen items, bedding, curtains, and furniture are all included. All sales are final — no returns or exchanges. Cash and credit cards are accepted, but we cannot take personal checks. Store hours during the sale are 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM every day, including weekends. Thank you to all our loyal customers over the years!

## Q1. How long has Parker's Home Goods been in business?
- A. 20 years
- B. 25 years
- C. 30 years
- D. 35 years
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The ad states the store 'is closing after 30 years of business.'

## Q2. What discount will be offered starting April 1?
- A. 30 percent off
- B. 40 percent off
- C. 50 percent off
- D. 60 percent off
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The ad says 'Starting April 1, prices will be reduced to 60 percent off.'
