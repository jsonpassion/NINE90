# English Class Registration

The Downtown Learning Center is now accepting registration for summer English classes. Classes begin on July 3 and end on August 25.

There are three levels: beginner, intermediate, and advanced. Classes meet twice a week, on Tuesdays and Thursdays, from 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. Each class has a maximum of 15 students. The fee is $200 for the full course.

To register, visit the center at 88 Main Street or call 555-0312. Registration closes on June 25. Textbooks are not included in the fee and can be purchased at the center bookstore.

## Q1. How often do classes meet each week?
- A. Once
- B. Twice
- C. Three times
- D. Every day
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says classes meet "twice a week, on Tuesdays and Thursdays."

## Q2. What is the registration deadline?
- A. June 25
- B. July 3
- C. August 25
- D. June 30
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The notice states "Registration closes on June 25."

## Q3. What is NOT included in the $200 fee?
- A. Class instruction
- B. Classroom materials
- C. Textbooks
- D. A class schedule
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says "Textbooks are not included in the fee and can be purchased at the center bookstore."
