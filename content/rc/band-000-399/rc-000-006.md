# Product Inquiry Email

From: Sarah Kim
To: support@brightlights.com
Subject: Question about desk lamp

Hello,

I saw your LED desk lamp on your website. The price is $45, and it looks very nice. I have a few questions. Does it come in other colors? I would like a white one. Also, how long does shipping take? I need it before May 1 for my new office. Can I return it if I do not like it? Thank you for your help. I look forward to your reply.

Best regards,
Sarah Kim

- A. A computer

- C. A desk
- D. A chair

- Explanation: Sarah writes "I saw your LED desk lamp on your website" and asks questions about it.

## Q2. What color does Sarah want?

- A. It is a gift for a friend.
