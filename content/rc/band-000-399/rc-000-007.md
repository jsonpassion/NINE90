# Green Market Spring Sale

Green Market is having a big spring sale! From March 20 to March 27, enjoy great prices on many items. All fruits and vegetables are 30% off. Fresh bread is buy one, get one free. Milk and juice are 20% off. The sale is at all three Green Market locations in downtown, Riverside, and Lakewood. Store hours during the sale are 7:00 A.M. to 10:00 P.M. Come early for the best selection! We accept cash and credit cards.

## Q1. How long does the sale last?
- A. Three days
- B. Five days
- C. One week
- D. Two weeks
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The sale runs from March 20 to March 27, which is one week.

## Q2. What is the discount on fruits and vegetables?

- C. 30% off
- D. 50% off
- Answer: C
