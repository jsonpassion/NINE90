# Community Garden Registration

SUNNYDALE COMMUNITY GARDEN — 2026 SEASON

Registration for garden plots is now open! The growing season runs from May 1 to October 31.

Plot sizes and fees:
- Small (5ft x 10ft): $30 per season
- Large (10ft x 10ft): $50 per season

Water is available at stations throughout the garden. Tools can be borrowed from the tool shed during garden hours (7:00 AM - 8:00 PM). Each gardener is responsible for maintaining their own plot and keeping pathways clear. Organic gardening methods are encouraged. No pesticides are allowed. Plots that are not maintained for 3 consecutive weeks will be reassigned. Register at the Parks Department office or online at sunnydale.gov/garden. Spaces are limited — only 40 plots available. Registration closes April 15 or when full.

## Q1. How much is a large plot?
- A. $20
- B. $30
- C. $50
- D. $40
- Answer: C
- Explanation: A large plot (10ft x 10ft) costs $50 per season.

## Q2. What is NOT allowed in the garden?
- A. Pesticides
- B. Borrowing tools
- C. Organic methods
- D. Watering in the morning
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The notice says no pesticides are allowed.

## Q3. What happens to unmaintained plots?
- A. The fee is doubled
- B. A warning letter is sent
- C. They are reassigned after 3 weeks
- D. Nothing happens
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Plots not maintained for 3 consecutive weeks will be reassigned.
