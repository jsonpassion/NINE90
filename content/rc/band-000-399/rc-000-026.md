# Swimming Pool Schedule

RIVERSIDE COMMUNITY POOL — SPRING SCHEDULE

The pool will reopen on April 1 for the spring season. Here is the new schedule:

Lap swimming: 6:00 AM - 8:00 AM (adults only)
Open swim: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Children's lessons: 12:30 PM - 1:30 PM (Tuesday and Thursday)
Water aerobics: 5:30 PM - 6:30 PM (Monday, Wednesday, Friday)

All swimmers must shower before entering the pool. Children under 8 must be with an adult. Season passes are $80 for adults and $40 for children under 12. Day passes are $5.

- D. 6:00 AM - 8:00 AM
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The schedule shows lap swimming from 6:00 AM to 8:00 AM.
