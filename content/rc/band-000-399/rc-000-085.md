# Free Health Check Event

Riverside Community Center is holding a free health check event on Saturday, April 22, from 9:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. The event is open to everyone in the community.

Doctors and nurses will check your blood pressure, weight, and vision. You can also get a free flu shot. No appointment is needed. Just come to the center at 45 River Road.

Children under 12 must come with a parent or guardian. For more information, call the center at 555-0234 or visit our website.

## Q1. What is the event about?
- A. A fitness class
- B. A free health check
- C. A doctor's conference
- D. A medicine sale
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice announces "a free health check event."

## Q2. What is NOT mentioned as part of the health check?
- A. Blood pressure check
- B. Vision check
- C. Flu shot
- D. Dental check
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Blood pressure, weight, vision, and flu shots are mentioned. Dental checks are not mentioned.

## Q3. Who must bring a parent or guardian?
- A. All visitors
- B. Senior citizens
- C. Children under 12
- D. First-time visitors
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states "Children under 12 must come with a parent or guardian."
