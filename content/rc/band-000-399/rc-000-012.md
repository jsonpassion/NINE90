# Building Maintenance Notice

To all residents of Maple Tower,

The elevators in the building will be checked and repaired on Wednesday, May 8 from 9:00 A.M. to 3:00 P.M. During this time, only one elevator will be working. Please expect longer wait times. Residents on floors 1 through 5 are encouraged to use the stairs if possible. We will also replace the lights in the lobby on the same day. The front door will remain open during the work. We apologize for any inconvenience and thank you for your patience.

— Maple Tower Management

## Q1. What will happen on May 8?
- A. The building will be closed.
- B. The elevators will be checked and repaired.
- C. New elevators will be installed.
- D. The stairs will be closed.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states "The elevators in the building will be checked and repaired on Wednesday, May 8."

## Q2. Who is encouraged to use the stairs?
- A. All residents
- B. Residents on floors 6 and above
- C. Residents on floors 1 through 5
- D. Building staff only
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says "Residents on floors 1 through 5 are encouraged to use the stairs if possible."

## Q3. What else will be replaced in the building?
- A. The front door
- B. The windows
- C. The carpet
- D. The lights in the lobby
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The notice mentions "We will also replace the lights in the lobby on the same day."
