# Warehouse Inventory Count

To: All Warehouse Staff
From: Operations Manager
Date: August 1

We will do our quarterly inventory count on Saturday, August 12. All warehouse staff must be present from 7:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m.

Please make sure all items in your area are properly labeled and organized before the count. Items without labels should be reported to your supervisor by August 10.

Lunch will be provided at noon. You will receive overtime pay for Saturday work. If you cannot come on August 12, please talk to your supervisor as soon as possible.

## Q1. When is the inventory count?
- A. August 1
- B. August 10
- C. August 12
- D. August 15
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo says the count is "on Saturday, August 12."

## Q2. What should staff do with unlabeled items?
- A. Report them to their supervisor
- B. Throw them away
- C. Label them themselves
- D. Move them to another area
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The memo says "Items without labels should be reported to your supervisor by August 10."

## Q3. What extra benefit will staff receive?
- A. A day off on Monday
- B. Overtime pay
- C. A gift card
- D. Extra vacation days
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo states "You will receive overtime pay for Saturday work."
