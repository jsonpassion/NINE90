# Company Picnic Invitation

Dear Team,

We are excited to announce our annual company picnic! This year, it will be held at Lakeside Park on Saturday, July 15, from 11:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Food and drinks will be provided. We will have barbecue, salads, and desserts. There will also be fun activities including volleyball, a sack race, and a photo booth.

Each employee may bring up to two family members. Please reply to this email by July 8 so we can plan for the right amount of food. Let us know if anyone in your group has food allergies.

We look forward to seeing everyone there!

## Q1. Where will the picnic be held?
- A. At Lakeside Park
- B. At the office
- C. At a restaurant
- D. At the company gym
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The email says the picnic "will be held at Lakeside Park."

## Q2. How many guests can each employee bring?
- A. One
- B. Two
- C. Three
- D. Unlimited
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email states "Each employee may bring up to two family members."

## Q3. Why should employees reply by July 8?
- A. To reserve a parking spot
- B. To choose their activities
- C. To help plan the right amount of food
- D. To get a discount
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The email says "Please reply to this email by July 8 so we can plan for the right amount of food."
