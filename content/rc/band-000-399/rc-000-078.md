# Pet Adoption Event

Pet Adoption Day — Sunnydale Animal Shelter

Come to our Pet Adoption Day on Saturday, April 12, from 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM! The Sunnydale Animal Shelter has over 40 dogs and cats looking for loving homes. All adoption fees are reduced to $50 for the event, which includes vaccinations, microchipping, and spay or neuter surgery. Every adopter will also receive a free bag of pet food and a starter kit with a leash, collar, and food bowl. Families with children are welcome, but please keep children close at all times. Our trained staff will help you find the perfect pet. The shelter is located at 900 River Road. No appointment needed — just walk in!

## Q1. What is included in the adoption fee?
- A. One year of free veterinary care
- B. Vaccinations, microchipping, and spay or neuter surgery
- C. A pet bed and toys
- D. Pet insurance for six months
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The ad states the fee 'includes vaccinations, microchipping, and spay or neuter surgery.'

## Q2. What extra items do adopters receive?
- A. A gift card
- B. A pet carrier and blanket
- C. A bag of pet food and a starter kit
- D. A training guide and DVD
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The ad says 'Every adopter will also receive a free bag of pet food and a starter kit.'

- A. Yes, you must book online
