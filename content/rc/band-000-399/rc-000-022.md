# Apartment Maintenance Notice

From: Building Management
To: All Residents
Date: March 20
Subject: Water Shut-Off on March 28

Dear Residents,

The water will be shut off on March 28 from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. Workers need to fix pipes on the 3rd floor. Please fill containers with water before 9:00 AM if you need it. The work should finish by 1:00 PM, but it may take until 2:00 PM. We are sorry for any trouble. If you have questions, call the management office at 555-0142.

## Q1. Why will the water be shut off?
- A. To clean the water tank
- B. To fix pipes on the 3rd floor
- C. To install new showers
- D. To test the fire system
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email says workers need to fix pipes on the 3rd floor.

## Q2. What should residents do before 9:00 AM?
- A. Leave the building
- B. Turn off all faucets
- C. Call the office
- D. Fill containers with water
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The email advises residents to fill containers with water before 9:00 AM.

## Q3. What is the latest the work might finish?
- A. 12:00 PM
- B. 1:00 PM
- C. 3:00 PM
- D. 2:00 PM
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The email says the work should finish by 1:00 PM but may take until 2:00 PM.
