# Volunteer Opportunity

From: coordinator@helpinghands.org
To: volunteers
Date: March 22
Subject: Volunteers Needed — Spring Cleanup Day

Dear Volunteers,

Our annual Spring Cleanup Day is on Saturday, April 5 from 9:00 AM to 1:00 PM. We need 30 volunteers to help clean up Miller Beach and the surrounding trails.

Tasks include picking up trash, planting flowers, and painting park benches. All supplies will be provided — just bring water and wear old clothes. Gloves and trash bags will be given out at check-in. Lunch will be served at 1:00 PM as a thank-you.

Volunteers will receive a Helping Hands T-shirt and 4 community service hours. Students needing volunteer hours for school are welcome. Please sign up by April 2 at helpinghands.org/spring. You must be at least 14 years old to participate. Younger volunteers may participate with a parent.

Hope to see you there!
