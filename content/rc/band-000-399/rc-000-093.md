# Store Return Policy

Thank you for shopping at Bright Electronics!

You may return or exchange any item within 30 days of purchase. Please keep your receipt. Items must be in their original packaging and in good condition.

The following items cannot be returned: headphones, opened software, and gift cards. Refunds will be given in the same form of payment used for the purchase. Cash purchases will receive a cash refund. Credit card purchases will be refunded to the card.

For returns, please visit the customer service desk at the front of the store. The desk is open during regular store hours, 10:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m., seven days a week.

## Q1. How many days do customers have to return an item?
- A. 30 days
- B. 14 days
- C. 7 days
- D. 60 days
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The policy says customers "may return or exchange any item within 30 days of purchase."

## Q2. Which item CANNOT be returned?
- A. A television
- B. A laptop
- C. Headphones
- D. A printer
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The policy lists "headphones, opened software, and gift cards" as items that cannot be returned.

## Q3. Where should customers go for returns?
- A. The manager's office
- B. Any checkout counter
- C. The customer service desk
- D. The electronics section
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The policy says "please visit the customer service desk at the front of the store."
