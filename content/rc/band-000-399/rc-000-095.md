# Library Hours Update

The Westfield Public Library will change its hours starting October 1.

New hours:
- Monday to Thursday: 9:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m.
- Friday and Saturday: 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
- Sunday: Closed

The library will also start a new children's story time every Wednesday at 10:00 a.m. This program is free and open to children ages 3 to 7. No registration is needed.

Books can be borrowed for up to three weeks. You may renew your books online or by calling 555-0288. Overdue books have a fee of 25 cents per day.

## Q1. When is the library closed?
- A. Monday
- B. Saturday
- C. Friday
- D. Sunday
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The new hours list shows "Sunday: Closed."

## Q2. How long can books be borrowed?
- A. One week

- D. Four weeks
- Answer: C

## Q3. What is the fee for overdue books?
- A. 10 cents per day
- B. 25 cents per day
- C. 50 cents per day
- D. $1 per day
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states "Overdue books have a fee of 25 cents per day."
