# Bus Route Change

CITY BUS SERVICE — ROUTE 14 UPDATE

Effective April 3, Bus Route 14 will follow a new path. The bus will no longer stop at Pine Street and 5th Avenue. Two new stops have been added: one at the Central Library and one at Riverside Park. The bus will run every 20 minutes from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM on weekdays. Weekend service runs every 30 minutes from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Monthly passes remain $55. Single ride tickets are $2.00. Senior citizens and students pay half price with valid ID. For the full new route map, visit citybus.com/route14.

## Q1. Which stop has been removed from Route 14?
- A. Central Library
- B. Pine Street and 5th Avenue
- C. Riverside Park
- D. City Hall
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says the bus will no longer stop at Pine Street and 5th Avenue.

## Q2. How often does the bus run on weekends?
- A. Every 15 minutes
- B. Every 30 minutes
- C. Every 20 minutes
- D. Every 45 minutes
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states weekend service runs every 30 minutes.

## Q3. How much does a student pay for a single ride?
- A. $1.00
- B. $0.50
- C. $1.50
- D. $2.00
- Answer: A
- Explanation: Single ride tickets are $2.00 and students pay half price, so $1.00.
