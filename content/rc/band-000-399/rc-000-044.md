# Summer Camp Registration

SUNSHINE KIDS SUMMER CAMP 2026

Give your children an amazing summer! Register now for Sunshine Kids Camp.

Ages: 6 to 14
Dates: June 16 - August 8 (8 weeks)
Hours: 8:30 AM - 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday
Location: Willow Creek Park

Activities include swimming, hiking, arts and crafts, sports, and nature classes. Campers bring their own lunch. Snacks and water are provided.

Fees: $175 per week or $1,200 for all 8 weeks (save $200). Sibling discount: 10% off the second child. A $50 deposit is required to hold your spot. Registration closes May 15 or when all spots are filled. Maximum capacity: 80 campers. Download the registration form at sunshinekidscamp.com or pick one up at the Parks Office.
