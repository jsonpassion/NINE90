# Fitness Center Rules

SUNRISE FITNESS CENTER — MEMBER RULES

1. All members must scan their card at the front desk to enter.
2. Workout shoes are required at all times. No sandals or bare feet.
3. Please wipe machines with the provided towels after each use.
4. Free weights must be returned to the rack when finished.
5. The maximum time on cardio machines is 30 minutes during peak hours (5 PM - 8 PM).
6. Personal trainers who are not employed by Sunrise Fitness may not work in the facility.
7. Lockers are for daily use only. Items left overnight will be removed.
8. Guest passes: Members may bring one guest per visit for $10.

## Q1. What must members do to enter the fitness center?
- A. Show their ID
- B. Pay a daily fee
- C. Sign a register
- D. Scan their card at the front desk
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Rule 1 states all members must scan their card at the front desk to enter.

## Q2. What is the maximum time on cardio machines during peak hours?
- A. 15 minutes
- B. 20 minutes
- C. 45 minutes
- D. 30 minutes
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Rule 5 states the maximum time on cardio machines is 30 minutes during peak hours.

## Q3. How much does a guest pass cost?
- A. $5
- B. $8
- C. $10
- D. $15
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Rule 8 says members may bring one guest per visit for $10.
