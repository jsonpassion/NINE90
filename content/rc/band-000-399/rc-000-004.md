# Holiday Schedule

To: All Employees
From: Human Resources Department
Date: March 7

Please review the upcoming holiday schedule for the second quarter of this year. The office will be closed on the following dates:

- Friday, April 18 — Spring Holiday
- Monday, May 5 — National Holiday
- Monday, May 26 — Memorial Day

Employees who are required to work on a holiday will receive double pay for that day. If you wish to take additional days off before or after a holiday, please submit a vacation request to your supervisor at least two weeks in advance.

Please note that all project deadlines remain the same, even during holiday weeks. Department managers should plan work schedules accordingly to ensure that important tasks are completed on time.

For questions about the holiday schedule or vacation requests, please contact the Human Resources Department at extension 3100.

## Q101. How many holidays are listed in the notice?
- A. Two
- B. Three
- C. Four
- D. Five
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice lists three holidays: April 18, May 5, and May 26.

## Q102. What will employees receive if they work on a holiday?
- A. An extra day off
- B. A bonus at the end of the year
- C. Double pay for that day
- D. Free lunch
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states "Employees who are required to work on a holiday will receive double pay for that day."

## Q103. How far in advance must vacation requests be submitted?
- A. One week
- B. At least two weeks
- C. One month
- D. Three days
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says employees should "submit a vacation request to your supervisor at least two weeks in advance."
