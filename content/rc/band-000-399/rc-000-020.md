# Meeting Room Booking Update

To: All Departments
From: Office Administration
Date: March 18
Subject: New Meeting Room Policy

Starting April 1, all meeting room bookings must be made through the new online system. Go to rooms.ourcompany.com and log in with your employee ID. You can book a room up to 2 weeks in advance. Each booking can be a maximum of 2 hours. If you need more time, you must make a second booking. Please cancel any booking you do not need at least 3 hours before the meeting time. Room B on the 4th floor now has a new projector and video call equipment.

## Q1. How should employees book meeting rooms starting April 1?
- A. By calling the front desk
- B. By sending an email
- C. Through the new online system
- D. By writing on a calendar
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo states "all meeting room bookings must be made through the new online system."

## Q2. How far in advance can a room be booked?
- A. 1 week
- B. 2 weeks
- C. 3 weeks
- D. 1 month
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo says "You can book a room up to 2 weeks in advance."

## Q3. What new equipment does Room B have?
- A. A whiteboard and markers
- B. A printer and scanner
- C. A projector and video call equipment
- D. A coffee machine and microwave
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo states "Room B on the 4th floor now has a new projector and video call equipment."
