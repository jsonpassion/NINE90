# Beginner Cooking Class

Learn to cook delicious meals at home! Valley Community Center is offering a 6-week cooking class for beginners.

When: Every Wednesday, 6:00 P.M. to 8:00 P.M.
Start Date: April 3
Where: Valley Community Center, Room 201
Cost: $120 for all 6 weeks (includes all ingredients)

Each week you will learn to make a different dish. No experience is needed. The class is limited to 15 students, so sign up early. You must be 16 years old or older to join. To register, visit our website at www.valleycc.org or call 555-0378.
