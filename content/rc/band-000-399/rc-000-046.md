# Recycling Program Update

IMPORTANT: CHANGES TO CURBSIDE RECYCLING

Starting April 7, Greenville will change its recycling pickup schedule. Recycling will now be collected on Wednesdays instead of Thursdays. Please place your blue bin at the curb by 7:00 AM on Wednesday mornings.

What goes in the blue bin:
- Paper, cardboard, newspapers
- Plastic bottles and containers (numbers 1-5)
- Metal cans (aluminum and steel)
- Glass bottles and jars

Do NOT include: plastic bags, food waste, electronics, or batteries. Electronics can be dropped off at the recycling center on Oak Street, open Monday-Saturday 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM. Contaminated bins will not be collected. Questions? Call 555-0123.

## Q1. When will recycling be collected under the new schedule?
- A. Tuesdays
- B. Fridays
- C. Thursdays
- D. Wednesdays
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The notice says recycling will now be collected on Wednesdays.

## Q2. Which item can go in the blue bin?
- A. Plastic bags
- B. Food waste
- C. Batteries
- D. Glass bottles
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Glass bottles and jars are listed as acceptable items for the blue bin.

## Q3. Where can electronics be dropped off?
- A. At City Hall
- B. At any grocery store
- C. At the fire station
- D. At the recycling center on Oak Street
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Electronics can be dropped off at the recycling center on Oak Street.
