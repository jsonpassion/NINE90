# Museum Visitor Guide

CITY HISTORY MUSEUM — VISITOR INFORMATION

Hours: Tuesday - Sunday, 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM. Closed Mondays.
Admission: Adults $12, Children (5-12) $6, Under 5 free, Seniors $8
Free admission every first Sunday of the month.

Current exhibits:
- "Life in the 1920s" — Main Hall (through May 30)
- "Local Artists" — Gallery B (through April 15)

Photography is allowed but flash is not permitted. Food and drinks are not allowed in exhibit areas. A café is located on the ground floor near the gift shop. Guided tours are available at 11:00 AM and 2:00 PM daily. Group tours of 10 or more must be booked in advance. Call 555-0456.

## Q1. How much is admission for a 10-year-old?
- A. Free
- B. $12
- C. $8
- D. $6
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Children ages 5-12 pay $6, so a 10-year-old pays $6.

## Q2. When can visitors get free admission?
- A. Every Monday
- B. The first Sunday of each month
- C. Every Sunday
- D. National holidays
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The sign says free admission every first Sunday of the month.

## Q3. What is NOT allowed in exhibit areas?
- A. Food and drinks
- B. Guided tours
- C. Photography
- D. Children under 5
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The sign states food and drinks are not allowed in exhibit areas.
