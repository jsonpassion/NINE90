# Holiday Schedule Notice

Dear valued customers,

Please note our holiday schedule for the month of May:

- May 1 (Wednesday) — Labor Day: CLOSED
- May 26 (Monday) — Memorial Day: CLOSED
- All other days: Normal hours (9:00 A.M. to 6:00 P.M.)

Our online store at www.harpergoods.com is open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. You can place orders online anytime and they will be shipped the next business day. For urgent orders during holidays, please use our express shipping option for an extra $8. Thank you for your continued support.

— Harper Goods

## Q1. How many days will the store be closed in May?
- A. 1
- B. 2
- C. 3
- D. 4
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The schedule shows two closed days: May 1 (Labor Day) and May 26 (Memorial Day).

## Q2. What are the normal store hours?
- A. 8:00 A.M. to 5:00 P.M.
- B. 9:00 A.M. to 6:00 P.M.
- C. 10:00 A.M. to 7:00 P.M.
- D. 9:00 A.M. to 9:00 P.M.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states normal hours are "9:00 A.M. to 6:00 P.M."

## Q3. How much extra does express shipping cost during holidays?
- A. $5
- B. $6
- C. $8
- D. $10
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says "use our express shipping option for an extra $8."
