# Water Shut-Off Notice

Water Service Interruption — Maplewood Apartments

To: All Residents, Buildings A and B
From: Building Management
Date: March 22

The water supply to Buildings A and B will be shut off on Wednesday, March 26, from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. This is necessary for pipe repairs on the third floor of Building A. We suggest that you store enough water for drinking and cooking before the shut-off. Bathrooms in the community center (Building C) will be available during this time. If the repairs are completed early, water service will be restored immediately. We apologize for the inconvenience. For urgent questions, please contact the building office at 555-0311 or email maintenance@maplewood.com.

## Q1. Which buildings will be affected?
- A. Buildings A and C
- B. Buildings A and B
- C. Buildings B and C
- D. All buildings
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states the water supply 'to Buildings A and B will be shut off.'

## Q2. How long will the water be off?
- A. 3 hours
- B. 4 hours
- C. 6 hours
- D. 8 hours
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The shut-off is from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM, which is 6 hours.

## Q3. Where can residents use bathrooms during the shut-off?
- A. Building A lobby
- B. Building B lobby
- C. Building C community center
- D. The nearby park
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says 'Bathrooms in the community center (Building C) will be available.'
