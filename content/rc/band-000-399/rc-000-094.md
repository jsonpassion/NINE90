# New Coffee Machine

To: All Staff
From: Administration
Date: March 15

We are happy to announce that a new coffee machine has been installed in the third-floor break room. It offers coffee, tea, and hot chocolate. All drinks are free for employees.

Please follow these simple rules:
1. Clean your cup after use.
2. Report any problems to the front desk.
3. Do not move the machine to another location.

The old coffee machine on the second floor will be removed next week. If you left a personal mug in the second-floor break room, please pick it up by March 22.

## Q1. Where is the new coffee machine?
- A. On the first floor
- B. On the second floor
- C. On the third floor
- D. In the lobby
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo says the machine "has been installed in the third-floor break room."

## Q2. What should employees do if the machine has a problem?
- A. Fix it themselves
- B. Call the manufacturer
- C. Send an email to IT
- D. Report it to the front desk
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The memo says to "Report any problems to the front desk."

## Q3. What will happen to the old coffee machine?
- A. It will be moved to the first floor
- B. It will be repaired
- C. It will be removed next week
- D. It will stay on the second floor
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo states "The old coffee machine on the second floor will be removed next week."
