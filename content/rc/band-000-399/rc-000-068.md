# Bus Route Change

City Bus Route 14 — Temporary Change

Starting March 25, Bus Route 14 will follow a different path due to road construction on Pine Street. The bus will not stop at Pine Street Station or Elm Avenue during this period. Instead, it will use Oak Boulevard and stop at Oak Boulevard Station and Central Park West. Passengers going to Pine Street can transfer to Route 7 at City Hall Station. The construction is expected to last four weeks. Normal service should return by April 22. Updated route maps are available at all bus stations and on the city transit website at www.citytransit.org. For more information, call the transit help line at 555-0199.

## Q1. Why is Bus Route 14 changing?
- A. Because of a new bus schedule
- B. Due to road construction on Pine Street
- C. Because of low ridership
- D. Due to a bus driver shortage
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states the change is 'due to road construction on Pine Street.'

## Q2. Where can passengers transfer to reach Pine Street?
- A. Oak Boulevard Station
- B. Central Park West
- C. City Hall Station
- D. Elm Avenue
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says 'Passengers going to Pine Street can transfer to Route 7 at City Hall Station.'

## Q3. When is normal service expected to return?
- A. April 1
- B. April 8
- C. April 15
- D. April 22
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The notice states 'Normal service should return by April 22.'
