# Apartment Building Notice

NOTICE TO ALL RESIDENTS — BUILDING 7

The building will undergo exterior painting from April 7 to April 18. Painters will work Monday through Friday, 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Please keep your windows closed during painting hours to avoid fumes. Balcony furniture should be moved inside or covered with plastic sheets by April 6. Plastic sheets are available at the management office free of charge. Parking spaces near the building may be blocked by scaffolding. Temporary parking is available in Lot C. The front entrance will remain open at all times. Side entrances may be temporarily blocked. Residents with health concerns about paint fumes should contact the office at 555-0445.

## Q1. How long will the painting take?
- A. About two weeks
- B. One week
- C. Three weeks
- D. One month
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The painting runs from April 7 to April 18, which is about two weeks.

## Q2. What should residents do with balcony furniture?
- A. Throw it away
- B. Bring it to the office
- C. Leave it as is
- D. Move it inside or cover it
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Residents should move balcony furniture inside or cover it with plastic sheets.

## Q3. Where can residents get plastic sheets?
- A. At the hardware store
- B. At the management office
- C. From the painters
- D. Online only
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Plastic sheets are available at the management office free of charge.
