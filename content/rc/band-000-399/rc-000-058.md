# Bike Rental Station

CITY BIKE RENTAL — STATION #12 (CENTRAL PARK)

How to rent:
1. Download the CityBike app or use the kiosk at the station.
2. Choose a bike and scan the QR code.
3. Return the bike to any CityBike station when done.

Rates:
- Single ride (up to 30 minutes): $2.50
- Day pass (unlimited 30-minute rides): $12
- Monthly membership: $25

Rides longer than 30 minutes are charged $1 for each extra 15 minutes. Helmets are available for $1 per rental. Report damaged bikes by pressing the red button on the bike's handlebars. Bikes must be returned by midnight. Lost or stolen bikes will be charged at $300. Bikes cannot be taken on buses or trains. Customer service: 555-0777.

## Q1. How much does a day pass cost?
- A. $12.00
- B. $8.00
- C. $2.50
- D. $25.00
- Answer: A
- Explanation: A day pass with unlimited 30-minute rides costs $12.

## Q3. How should damaged bikes be reported?
- A. Call customer service
- B. Return the bike and tell staff
- C. Report through the app only
