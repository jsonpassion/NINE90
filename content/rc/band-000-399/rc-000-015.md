# City Library Rules

Welcome to City Library! Please follow these rules:

1. Library hours: Monday to Saturday, 9:00 A.M. to 8:00 P.M.
2. You may borrow up to 5 books at a time.
3. Books must be returned within 14 days.
4. Late returns cost $0.50 per day per book.
5. Please keep your phone on silent mode.
6. Food is not allowed, but you may bring water.
7. Free Wi-Fi is available. Ask the front desk for the password.

A library card is free for all city residents. To get a card, bring a photo ID and proof of address to the front desk.

## Q1. How many books can you borrow at one time?
- A. 3
- B. 5
- C. 7
- D. 10
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Rule 2 states "You may borrow up to 5 books at a time."

## Q2. What is the late fee per book per day?
- A. $0.25
- B. $0.50
- C. $1.00
- D. $2.00
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Rule 4 states "Late returns cost $0.50 per day per book."

## Q3. What do you need to get a library card?
- A. A student ID only
- B. A phone number and email
- C. A photo ID and proof of address
- D. A recommendation letter
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says "bring a photo ID and proof of address to the front desk."
