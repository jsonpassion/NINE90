# Company Picnic Invitation

From: HR Department
To: All Staff
Subject: Annual Company Picnic

Dear team,

You are invited to our annual company picnic on Saturday, July 15 at Riverside Park. The event starts at 11:00 A.M. and ends at 4:00 P.M. There will be free food, drinks, and games for everyone. You may bring your family members. Please sign up by July 10 so we can prepare enough food. To sign up, reply to this email with the number of people attending. Parking is free at the park. We hope to see you there!

Best,
Human Resources

## Q1. When is the company picnic?
- A. July 10
- B. July 12
- C. July 15
- D. July 20
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The email states the picnic is "on Saturday, July 15."

## Q2. What should employees do to sign up?
- A. Call the HR office
- B. Visit the park
- C. Fill out a form online
- D. Reply to the email
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The email says "To sign up, reply to this email with the number of people attending."

## Q3. Who can employees bring to the picnic?
- A. Only coworkers
- B. Their family members
- C. Their pets
- D. No one else
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email states "You may bring your family members."
