# New Security Procedures

To: All Staff
From: Security Department
Date: March 22
Subject: Updated Building Security Procedures

Effective April 1, the following security changes will take effect:

1. All employees must wear their ID badge visibly at all times in the building.
2. Visitors must sign in at the front desk and receive a visitor badge. The badge must be returned when leaving.
3. Deliveries will only be accepted at the loading dock between 8:00 AM and 4:00 PM.
4. The side entrance on Park Street will be locked after 6:00 PM. Use the main entrance after that time.
5. All employees leaving after 8:00 PM must sign out at the security desk.
6. If you see anyone without a badge, please report it to security at extension 1100.

These measures are for everyone's safety. Thank you for your cooperation.

## Q1. What must visitors do when they arrive?
- A. Show a photo ID only
- B. Wait in the parking lot
- C. Sign in and receive a visitor badge
- D. Call their host employee
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Visitors must sign in at the front desk and receive a visitor badge.

## Q2. When will the side entrance be locked?
- A. After 6:00 PM
- B. After 5:00 PM
- C. After 7:00 PM
- D. After 8:00 PM
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The side entrance on Park Street will be locked after 6:00 PM.

## Q3. What should employees do if they see someone without a badge?
- A. Report it to security at extension 1100
- B. Call the police
- C. Ask the person to leave
- D. Ignore it
- Answer: A
- Explanation: Employees should report anyone without a badge to security at extension 1100.
