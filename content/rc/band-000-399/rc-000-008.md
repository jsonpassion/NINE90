# Parking Lot Notice

Attention all tenants,

The parking lot behind the building will be repaired starting Monday, June 5. The work will take about two weeks. During this time, the back parking lot will be closed. Please use the front parking lot on Oak Street instead. The front lot has 50 spaces and is open 24 hours. Please remember to display your parking permit on your dashboard. Cars without a permit may be towed. We are sorry for the inconvenience. If you have questions, please call the management office at 555-0192.

## Q1. Why will the back parking lot be closed?
- A. It is full.
- B. It is being repaired.
- C. It is too small.
- D. There is a new building.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says "The parking lot behind the building will be repaired."

## Q2. How long will the repair work take?
- A. One week
- B. About two weeks
- C. One month
- D. Three days
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states "The work will take about two weeks."

## Q3. What may happen to cars without a permit?
- A. They will get a warning.
- B. They will be moved to the back lot.
- C. They may be towed.
- D. They will receive a discount.
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice warns "Cars without a permit may be towed."
