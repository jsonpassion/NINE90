# Pharmacy Hours and Services

Welcome to Green Cross Pharmacy. We are located at 22 Elm Street, next to the post office.

Our hours are:
- Monday to Friday: 8:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m.
- Saturday: 9:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m.
- Sunday: 10:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

We offer prescription medicines, over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and personal care products. We also provide free blood pressure checks every Tuesday afternoon from 2:00 p.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Need a refill? You can order online at www.greencrosspharm.com or call us at 555-0375. Orders placed before 2:00 p.m. will be ready the same day.
