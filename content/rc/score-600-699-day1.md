# Office Relocation Notice

All employees are informed that the Seoul branch office will relocate to the Gangnam Business Tower on April 15. IT support will transfer all equipment over the weekend prior to the move. Normal operations will resume Monday morning. Employees are advised to back up personal files before Friday.

## Q131. What will happen over the weekend?
- A. The branch office will reopen to customers.
- B. The IT team will move office equipment.
- C. Team leaders will submit participant lists.
- D. Employees will receive revised tracking numbers.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The passage states weekend equipment transfer.

## Q132. What should employees do before Friday?
- A. Submit vacation requests
- B. Back up personal files
- C. Update their contact information
- D. Attend a training session
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Employees are advised to back up personal files.

## Q133. In the notice, 'relocate' is closest in meaning to:
- A. hire
- B. repair
- C. move
- D. decorate
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Relocate means to move to a new location.

# Quarterly Sales Report

Third-quarter sales exceeded projections by 12%, driven primarily by strong demand in the Asian market. The marketing team's digital campaign contributed to a 25% increase in online orders compared to the previous quarter. Management has approved additional budget allocation for Q4 to sustain the growth momentum.

## Q134. By how much did sales exceed projections?
- A. 8%
- B. 12%
- C. 25%
- D. 30%
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Sales exceeded projections by 12%.

## Q135. What contributed to increased online orders?
- A. A price reduction
- B. The digital marketing campaign
- C. New product launches
- D. Store closures
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The digital campaign drove 25% increase in online orders.

## Q136. What has management decided for Q4?
- A. Reduce staff
- B. Approve additional budget
- C. Close the Asian market
- D. Delay the campaign
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Additional budget allocation approved for Q4.
