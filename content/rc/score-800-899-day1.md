# Contract Renewal Brief

Notwithstanding temporary cost pressure from the vendor's revised pricing structure, the procurement committee approved the contract renewal for an additional three-year term. The decision was predicated on subsequent performance audits that demonstrated measurable gains in operational efficiency, lower risk exposure, and improved vendor responsiveness. A provision requiring annual performance benchmarking has been added to the renewed agreement.

## Q161. Why did the committee approve the renewal?
- A. Lower office rent
- B. Performance gains and lower risk exposure
- C. A new hiring plan
- D. Marketing discounts from the vendor
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Subsequent audits showed gains in efficiency and lower risk.

## Q162. What does the passage imply about vendor costs?
- A. They were permanently reduced
- B. They were temporarily higher than before
- C. They were unknown to the committee
- D. They were irrelevant to the decision
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Temporary cost pressure implies costs were higher.

## Q163. What new provision was added to the contract?
- A. A price freeze clause
- B. Annual performance benchmarking
- C. Quarterly payment terms
- D. A non-compete agreement
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Annual performance benchmarking provision was added.

# Merger Integration Update

The integration of Apex Technologies into our organizational structure is proceeding ahead of the projected timeline. Key milestones achieved include the consolidation of IT infrastructure, harmonization of employee benefit packages, and the establishment of cross-functional advisory panels. Stringent due diligence protocols remain in effect to ensure regulatory compliance throughout the transition period.

## Q164. How is the integration progressing?
- A. Behind schedule
- B. On schedule
- C. Ahead of the projected timeline
- D. It has been postponed
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Proceeding ahead of the projected timeline.

## Q165. What has NOT been mentioned as a completed milestone?
- A. IT infrastructure consolidation
- B. Employee benefit harmonization
- C. Office relocation
- D. Cross-functional advisory panels
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Office relocation is not mentioned in the passage.

## Q166. In this context, 'stringent' is closest in meaning to:
- A. flexible
- B. strict
- C. optional
- D. temporary
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Stringent means very strict or demanding.
