# Marketing Coordinator Position Available

Greenfield Industries is seeking a motivated Marketing Coordinator to join our growing team at our downtown headquarters. This is a full-time position with an annual salary range of $45,000 to $55,000, depending on experience.

The ideal candidate will have a bachelor's degree in marketing, communications, or a related field, along with at least two years of professional experience. Strong written and verbal communication skills are essential, and familiarity with social media platforms and digital marketing tools is preferred.

Key responsibilities include coordinating marketing campaigns, managing the company's social media accounts, assisting with event planning, and preparing monthly performance reports. The position also involves working closely with the sales department to develop promotional materials.

Greenfield Industries offers a comprehensive benefits package including health insurance, dental coverage, a retirement savings plan, and 20 days of paid vacation per year. Interested applicants should submit a resume and cover letter through our careers portal at www.greenfieldindustries.com/careers by April 15, 2026.

## Q1. What is the minimum experience required for this position?
- A. One year
- B. Two years
- C. Three years
- D. Five years
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The advertisement states the ideal candidate should have "at least two years of professional experience."

## Q2. Which of the following is NOT listed as a responsibility of the Marketing Coordinator?

- B. Preparing monthly reports
- C. Hiring new team members
- D. Assisting with event planning
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Hiring new team members is not mentioned among the key responsibilities, which include coordinating campaigns, managing social media, assisting with events, and preparing reports.

- A. By sending an email to the HR department

- Answer: C
- Explanation: The advertisement instructs applicants to "submit a resume and cover letter through our careers portal at www.greenfieldindustries.com/careers."
