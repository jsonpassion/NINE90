# Product Return Policy Update

Dear Valued Customers,

Effective July 1, Henderson Electronics is updating its product return policy. Please review the following changes carefully.

Previously, all products could be returned within 14 days of purchase. Under the new policy, the return period has been extended to 30 days for most items. However, opened software, headphones, and clearance items are excluded from this extension and must still be returned within 14 days.

To process a return, customers must present the original receipt or order confirmation number. Items must be in their original packaging and in unused condition. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days after the return is approved.

For online purchases, customers may initiate a return through our website by visiting www.hendersonelectronics.com/returns and entering their order number. A prepaid shipping label will be provided at no additional cost.

If you have any questions about the updated policy, please contact our customer service team at 1-800-555-0192 or visit any Henderson Electronics store location.
