# Conference Booth Reservation

From: David Park, Marketing Director
To: Events Coordination Team
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: TechConnect Conference Booth Reservation

Dear Team,

I have secured a premium booth at the TechConnect Conference, which will be held at the Metropolitan Convention Center from May 19 to May 21. Our booth number is P-205, located in the main exhibition hall near the keynote stage. This is a significant upgrade from last year's standard booth in the side corridor.

The premium booth package costs $8,500 and includes a 20-foot by 15-foot space, electricity and Wi-Fi access, two 55-inch display screens, a storage closet, and four exhibitor passes. Additional exhibitor passes can be purchased for $175 each. Based on last year's results, where we generated 340 qualified leads from a smaller booth, I expect this year's premium placement to yield at least 500 leads.

I need the following from each team by April 11: Marketing should prepare updated product brochures and demo materials. The design team should create the booth graphics and backdrop. Sales should confirm which four representatives will staff the booth across the three days. HR should coordinate travel and hotel arrangements for all attendees.

Our total conference budget is $18,000, covering the booth, travel, marketing materials, and a client dinner on the evening of May 20. Please submit itemized budget estimates by April 4.

Thank you for your prompt attention to these tasks.
