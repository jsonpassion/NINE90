# Department Restructuring Plan

To: All Employees — Sales and Marketing Division
From: VP of Operations, Robert Hanson
Date: March 20, 2026
Subject: Restructuring of Sales and Marketing Teams

After careful evaluation of our organizational structure, we have decided to merge the sales and marketing departments into a single unified division called the Revenue Growth Department. This change will take effect on May 1, 2026.

The primary goal of this restructuring is to improve coordination between sales and marketing activities, which have historically operated independently. By bringing these teams together, we expect to reduce project duplication, streamline communication, and create a more efficient workflow for product launches and campaigns.

Jennifer Walsh has been appointed as the new Senior Director of the Revenue Growth Department. She brings 12 years of experience in both sales and marketing leadership. All current team leads will retain their positions, and no layoffs are planned as part of this restructuring.

Over the next six weeks, a transition committee will work with both teams to realign reporting structures and update project workflows. Town hall meetings will be held on April 7 and April 14 to address employee questions and concerns. Individual team meetings will be scheduled during the week of April 21 to discuss specific role changes.

We understand that organizational changes can be unsettling, and we are committed to making this transition as smooth as possible.
