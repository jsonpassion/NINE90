# Employee Wellness Program Update

Effective May 1, Summit Corporation will expand its employee wellness program to include mental health support services. In addition to the existing gym membership reimbursement and annual health screenings, employees will now have access to confidential counseling sessions at no cost.

Each employee is entitled to six free counseling sessions per year through our partner, Wellspring Counseling Services. Sessions can be scheduled online through the benefits portal or by calling Wellspring directly at 555-0488. Both in-person and virtual sessions are available.

Employees who participate in at least three wellness activities during the year will receive a $200 bonus on their December paycheck. Qualifying activities include gym visits, counseling sessions, health screenings, and the company wellness workshop series held every quarter.

For more details about the expanded program, attend the information session on April 25 at noon in the main auditorium. Light refreshments will be served.

## Q1. What new service is being added to the wellness program?
- A. Gym membership discounts
- B. Annual health screenings
- C. Mental health counseling sessions
- D. Nutrition planning services
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says the program will "expand to include mental health support services" with "confidential counseling sessions."
