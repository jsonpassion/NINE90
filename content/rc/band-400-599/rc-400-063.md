# Office Renovation Notice

MEMORANDUM

To: All Employees — 3rd Floor
From: Facilities Management
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: Office Renovation — 3rd Floor West Wing

We are writing to inform you that renovation work on the 3rd floor west wing will begin on Monday, April 7, and is expected to be completed by Friday, May 2. The renovation includes new carpeting, updated lighting, and the installation of additional power outlets to support modern workstation configurations.

During the renovation period, employees in the west wing will be temporarily relocated to the 5th floor. Temporary workstations have been arranged in rooms 501 through 510. Your IT equipment will be moved over the weekend of April 5-6 by our technical support team. Please ensure that all personal items are packed in the boxes provided by Friday, April 4, at 5:00 PM.

The east wing of the 3rd floor will remain open and fully operational throughout the renovation. However, noise levels may be higher than usual during working hours. Noise-canceling headphones will be available for loan from the Facilities desk on the 1st floor.

We appreciate your patience during this improvement project. If you have specific questions about your temporary workspace assignment, please contact David Park at extension 3320.
