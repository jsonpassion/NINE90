# Vendor Performance Review

To: Procurement Team
From: Laura Bennett, Director of Operations
Date: March 21, 2026
Subject: Annual Vendor Performance Review Results

The annual vendor performance review for our top 10 suppliers has been completed. I am sharing the key findings to guide our procurement decisions for the upcoming fiscal year.

Pinnacle Office Supplies received the highest overall rating of 4.7 out of 5, with particularly strong marks for delivery reliability and product quality. They have been our primary office supplies vendor for six years and continue to offer competitive pricing. I recommend renewing their three-year contract at current terms.

GreenTech Printing, however, received a rating of 2.8 out of 5, the lowest among all reviewed vendors. Multiple departments reported delayed deliveries, averaging 5 business days past the agreed timeline, and a defect rate of 7 percent on printed materials. This is significantly above the acceptable threshold of 2 percent. I recommend issuing a formal performance improvement notice and beginning the process of identifying alternative printing vendors.

DataStream Technologies received a rating of 3.9 out of 5 for their cloud hosting services. While their technical support has improved since last year, their pricing remains 15 percent above the market average. I suggest renegotiating the contract terms before the renewal date in June.

Please schedule a meeting with me before April 5 to discuss next steps for each vendor relationship.

## Q1. Which vendor received the highest rating?
- A. GreenTech Printing
- B. Pinnacle Office Supplies
- C. DataStream Technologies
- D. The memo does not specify
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Pinnacle Office Supplies received the highest overall rating of 4.7 out of 5.

- C. Poor technical support
- D. Delayed deliveries and high defect rate
- Answer: D
- Explanation: GreenTech had delayed deliveries and a 7% defect rate, well above the 2% threshold.
