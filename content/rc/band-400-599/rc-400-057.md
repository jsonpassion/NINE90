# Internship Program Launch

SUMMER INTERNSHIP PROGRAM 2026 — ACCEPTING APPLICATIONS

Crestline Technologies is launching its inaugural Summer Internship Program for university students interested in gaining hands-on experience in the technology industry. The program will run for 10 weeks from June 9 through August 15, 2026.

We are offering 12 paid internship positions across four departments: Software Engineering (4 positions), Data Science (3 positions), Product Management (2 positions), and UX Design (3 positions). Interns will work full-time (40 hours per week) and will be compensated at $28 per hour. Housing assistance of $2,000 is available for interns relocating from outside the metropolitan area.

Each intern will be paired with a senior mentor and will work on real projects that contribute to our product roadmap. The program includes weekly learning sessions with company leaders, a mid-program presentation to the department, and a final showcase to the executive team. High-performing interns will be considered for full-time positions upon graduation.

Eligibility: Applicants must be currently enrolled in a bachelor's or master's degree program at an accredited university, with an expected graduation date between December 2026 and June 2028. A minimum GPA of 3.0 on a 4.0 scale is required.

Applications must be submitted by April 25, 2026, through our careers page at crestlinetech.com/internships. Selected candidates will participate in two rounds of interviews: a phone screen followed by a virtual panel interview. Decisions will be communicated by May 16.
