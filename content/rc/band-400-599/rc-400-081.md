# Quarterly Performance Review Schedule

To: Department Managers
From: Helen Park, Director of Human Resources
Date: April 2

As we approach the end of the second quarter, I would like to remind all department managers that quarterly performance reviews must be completed by June 15. Each manager is responsible for scheduling individual meetings with their team members during the first two weeks of June.

The updated review form is now available on the HR portal. This year, we have added a new section for employee self-assessment, which should be completed by each employee before the review meeting. Managers should distribute the self-assessment form to their teams no later than May 20 to allow adequate preparation time.

Please note that all completed review forms must be submitted electronically through the HR portal. Paper submissions will no longer be accepted. If you experience any technical issues with the portal, contact the IT support team at extension 3350.

A training session on conducting effective performance reviews will be held on May 10 at 2:00 p.m. in the training center. Attendance is recommended for all managers, especially those who are new to the review process.

## Q1. What is the deadline for completing performance reviews?
- A. April 2
- B. May 20
- C. June 30
- D. June 15
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The email states that "quarterly performance reviews must be completed by June 15."

## Q2. What is new about this year's review process?
- A. Reviews will be done monthly instead of quarterly
- B. A self-assessment section has been added
- C. All reviews must be conducted in person
- D. External consultants will lead the reviews
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email mentions "we have added a new section for employee self-assessment."

## Q3. How must completed review forms be submitted?
- A. By email to the HR director
- B. In person at the HR office
- C. Electronically through the HR portal
- D. By mail to the main office
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The email says "all completed review forms must be submitted electronically through the HR portal."
