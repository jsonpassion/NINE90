# Annual Company Picnic Announcement

Attention all employees! Riverside Technologies is pleased to announce our annual company picnic, scheduled for Saturday, May 10, 2026, from 11:00 a.m. to 4:00 p.m. This year's event will be held at Lakewood Park, located at 250 Lakeshore Drive.

Employees are welcome to bring family members and up to two additional guests. A variety of food and beverages will be provided, including grilled items, salads, desserts, and both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks. Vegetarian and gluten-free options will also be available.

Activities planned for the day include a volleyball tournament, a three-legged race, a treasure hunt for children, and live music performed by a local band. There will also be a raffle drawing with prizes such as gift cards, electronics, and an extra vacation day.

To help us plan for food and activities, please register by April 25 using the sign-up form on the company intranet. Indicate the number of guests you plan to bring and any dietary restrictions. Free parking is available at the park, and a shuttle bus will run from the main office every 30 minutes starting at 10:30 a.m.
