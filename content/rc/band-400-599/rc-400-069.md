# Travel Policy Reminder

MEMORANDUM

To: All Traveling Employees
From: Finance Department
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: Reminder — Business Travel Expense Policy

This is a reminder of the company's business travel expense policy. With the upcoming conference season, please review the following guidelines to ensure timely reimbursement of your travel expenses.

All business travel must be pre-approved by your direct supervisor and the Finance Department. Approval requests should be submitted at least 10 business days before the travel date. Flights should be booked in economy class for domestic travel. Business class may be approved for international flights exceeding eight hours. Hotel expenses must not exceed $200 per night in major cities and $150 per night in other locations.

Meal expenses are reimbursed up to $75 per day and do not require individual receipts for meals under $25. However, any single meal exceeding $25 must have an itemized receipt. Entertainment expenses such as client dinners require prior approval and a written explanation of the business purpose.

Expense reports must be submitted within 14 days of returning from travel. Reports submitted after 30 days may not be reimbursed. All reports should be submitted through the TravelEase platform, which is accessible from the company intranet.

Please contact the Finance Department at finance@company.com or extension 4100 for any questions regarding this policy.
