# Client Appreciation Event

From: Marketing Department
To: All Client-Facing Staff
Date: March 25, 2026
Subject: Annual Client Appreciation Event — May 8

Dear Team,

Our annual Client Appreciation Event will be held on Thursday, May 8, from 6:00 PM to 9:00 PM at the Lakeview Grand Ballroom. This year's event will celebrate our company's 25th anniversary and recognize our most valued client partnerships.

The evening will include a cocktail reception, a three-course dinner, a brief presentation by CEO Margaret Torres highlighting key milestones, and live music by the Sterling Jazz Quartet. Each attending client will receive a commemorative gift package valued at approximately $75.

We have a budget for up to 200 guests, including 120 client representatives and 80 company employees. Each department has been allocated a specific number of client invitations based on account size and relationship history. Please refer to the allocation list attached to this email.

Invitations should be sent by April 10, and RSVPs are due by April 25. Custom invitation templates are available on the marketing shared drive. All client-facing staff are expected to attend unless they have a scheduling conflict, in which case they should notify their manager by April 15.

Please contact events@company.com for logistical questions or speak with your department head about invitation allocations.
