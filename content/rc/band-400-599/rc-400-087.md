# Quality Control Procedure Update

To: Production Team Leaders
From: Diane Cho, Quality Assurance Manager
Date: February 28

Following last month's review of our product return data, we are implementing updated quality control procedures effective March 15. These changes are aimed at reducing the defect rate, which currently stands at 2.3 percent, to below 1.5 percent by the end of the quarter.

The most significant change is the addition of a second inspection point on the assembly line. Currently, products are inspected only at the final stage. Under the new procedure, an initial inspection will take place after the component assembly phase, allowing defective parts to be identified and replaced before further processing.

Each team leader is required to attend a two-hour training session during the week of March 8. Training schedules have been posted on the production floor bulletin board. Please ensure that at least one inspector from your team also attends the session.

Questions about the updated procedures can be directed to my office at extension 5510 or by email at d.cho@company.com.

## Q1. What is the current product defect rate?
- A. 2.3 percent
- B. 1.5 percent
- C. 1.0 percent
- D. 3.0 percent
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The memo states "the defect rate, which currently stands at 2.3 percent."

## Q2. What is the main change in the quality control process?
- A. A new packing method will be introduced
- B. A second inspection point will be added
- C. All products will be tested by an outside company
- D. Inspections will happen only once a week
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo says "the most significant change is the addition of a second inspection point on the assembly line."

## Q3. Who is required to attend the training session?
- A. All production workers
- B. Only quality inspectors
- C. Team leaders and at least one inspector per team
- D. Only the quality assurance manager
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo says "Each team leader is required to attend" and they should "ensure that at least one inspector from your team also attends."
