# Office Relocation Announcement

INTERTECH SOLUTIONS — OFFICE RELOCATION NOTICE

Intertech Solutions is pleased to announce that our headquarters will be moving to a new location effective May 1, 2026. After 15 years at our current address on Pine Avenue, we have outgrown our space and will be relocating to the Meridian Business Park at 500 Technology Drive, Suite 800.

The new office features 40 percent more floor space, a modern open-plan layout, dedicated meeting rooms with videoconferencing capabilities, and an employee wellness center including a fitness room and quiet zones. Underground parking will be available for all employees at no charge, replacing the current paid surface lot arrangement.

The transition will occur over the weekend of April 26-27. Employees are asked to pack their personal items and label their boxes by Thursday, April 24 at 5:00 PM. The moving company will handle all furniture and equipment. IT will ensure that all computers and phone systems are operational by Monday morning.

Please update your business cards and email signatures with the new address. Clients and vendors have been notified separately. Questions should be directed to the Relocation Committee at relocation@intertech.com.

## Q1. Why is Intertech Solutions moving?
- A. To reduce operating costs
- B. Because the lease has expired
- C. Because they have outgrown their current space
- D. To be closer to major clients
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The announcement says they have outgrown their space at the current address.

## Q2. What is a benefit of the new office that the current office does not offer?
- A. Paid surface parking
- B. An open-plan layout only
- C. Free underground parking
- D. Larger individual offices
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The new office has free underground parking, replacing the current paid surface lot.

## Q3. By when must employees pack their personal items?
- A. April 22 at noon
- B. April 24 at 5:00 PM
- C. April 26 at midnight
- D. May 1 at 9:00 AM
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Employees are asked to pack by Thursday, April 24 at 5:00 PM.
