# Company Shuttle Service

MEMORANDUM

To: All Employees
From: Administration Department
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: New Employee Shuttle Service

We are pleased to announce the launch of a free employee shuttle service starting April 7, 2026. The shuttle will operate between three pickup locations and the main office to make commuting easier and support our green transportation initiative.

Route 1 departs from Central Station at 7:30 AM and 8:15 AM. Route 2 departs from Westfield Mall at 7:45 AM and 8:30 AM. Route 3 departs from Riverside Park at 7:15 AM and 8:00 AM. Return shuttles depart from the main office at 5:30 PM and 6:15 PM for all routes. Each shuttle can accommodate 25 passengers on a first-come, first-served basis.

To use the shuttle, employees must register through the CommuteSmart app, available for download on the company intranet. Registration is required for planning purposes but does not guarantee a seat. Employees are encouraged to arrive at the pickup location at least five minutes before departure time.

The shuttle service will operate on a trial basis for three months. Its continuation beyond July will depend on ridership levels. We need an average of 15 riders per route to sustain the service.

We encourage all employees to give the shuttle a try. For questions, contact transportation@company.com.
