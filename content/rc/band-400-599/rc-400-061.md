# Employee Training Program

From: Human Resources Department
To: All Department Managers
Date: March 24, 2026
Subject: Mandatory Compliance Training — Q2 Schedule

Dear Managers,

This email is to inform you that the annual compliance training program for all employees will be conducted during the second quarter of this year. The training covers workplace safety, data privacy regulations, and anti-harassment policies. Each session lasts approximately three hours and will be offered on multiple dates to accommodate different schedules.

The training will be delivered in two formats. Employees at the main office can attend in-person sessions in Conference Room A on April 7, April 14, or April 21. Remote employees will complete the training through our online learning platform, which will be available starting April 1. All employees must complete the training by May 31. Managers are responsible for ensuring that every member of their team is registered for a session.

Employees who completed last year's training are not exempt; this year's program includes updated content reflecting recent changes to data protection laws. Please review the attached schedule and confirm your team's registration by March 31.

If you have any questions, please contact Sarah Kim in HR at extension 5540.

## Q1. What is the deadline for completing the training?
- A. March 31
- B. April 21
- C. May 31
- D. June 30
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The email states 'All employees must complete the training by May 31.'

## Q2. How can remote employees take the training?
- A. By traveling to the main office
- B. Through the online learning platform
- C. By attending a video conference
- D. By reading a printed manual
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email says 'Remote employees will complete the training through our online learning platform.'

## Q3. Why must employees who trained last year participate again?
- A. Their certificates have expired.
- B. The program includes updated content on data protection laws.
- C. The company changed its training provider.
- D. New employees need mentors during training.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email states the program 'includes updated content reflecting recent changes to data protection laws.'
