# Partnership Proposal

From: Angela Rivera, Business Development Director
To: Kevin Zhao, CEO, NovaTech Solutions
Date: March 18, 2026
Subject: Strategic Partnership Proposal

Dear Mr. Zhao,

I am writing on behalf of Crestview Consulting to propose a strategic partnership between our two companies. After observing NovaTech's impressive growth in the data analytics sector, we believe a collaboration would create significant value for both organizations.

Crestview Consulting specializes in business strategy and operational efficiency for technology companies. We currently serve 85 clients across North America and Europe. Our team of 120 consultants has helped companies achieve an average cost reduction of 18 percent within the first year of engagement.

We propose a partnership in which Crestview provides strategic consulting services to NovaTech's enterprise clients as a bundled offering with your analytics platform. In return, NovaTech would serve as our preferred data analytics provider for our consulting projects. This arrangement would allow both companies to expand their service offerings without the cost of building new capabilities internally.

To discuss this proposal further, I would like to invite you and your team to a meeting at our headquarters in Chicago during the week of April 14. We will cover travel and accommodation expenses for up to four attendees from your organization.

Please let me know if this proposal interests you, and I will have my assistant coordinate the meeting details.

## Q1. What does Crestview Consulting specialize in?
- A. Data analytics and software development
- B. Business strategy and operational efficiency
- C. Financial auditing and tax services
- D. Human resources and recruitment
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email states "Crestview Consulting specializes in business strategy and operational efficiency for technology companies."

## Q2. What is the proposed arrangement between the two companies?
- A. Crestview will acquire NovaTech.
- B. NovaTech will hire Crestview consultants as employees.
- C. Crestview will offer consulting bundled with NovaTech's platform, and NovaTech will be Crestview's preferred analytics provider.
- D. Both companies will merge into a single entity.
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The email proposes "Crestview provides strategic consulting services to NovaTech's enterprise clients as a bundled offering" while "NovaTech would serve as our preferred data analytics provider."

- A. Two
