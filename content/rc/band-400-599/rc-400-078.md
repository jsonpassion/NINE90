# Catering Service Proposal

From: Daniel Reyes, Catering Manager, FreshBite Catering
To: Rachel Park, Office Administrator
Date: March 21, 2026
Subject: Corporate Lunch Catering Proposal

Dear Ms. Park,

Thank you for your inquiry regarding daily lunch catering for your office. Based on our discussion, I am pleased to submit the following proposal for your consideration.

FreshBite Catering offers three service tiers. The Standard Package, at $12 per person, includes a rotating menu of sandwiches, salads, and soup, with vegetarian options available daily. The Premium Package, at $18 per person, adds hot entrees such as grilled chicken, pasta, and stir-fry dishes, along with a dessert selection. The Executive Package, at $25 per person, features chef-prepared meals with seasonal menus, artisan bread, premium desserts, and complimentary beverages.

For your estimated group of 60 employees, we can offer a 10 percent volume discount on any package. Setup and cleanup are included in all packages. We provide all plates, utensils, and napkins using eco-friendly disposable materials. Dietary accommodations for allergies and religious requirements can be arranged with 48 hours' notice.

Our minimum commitment is three months, with a 30-day cancellation clause. We can begin service as early as April 14, pending a signed agreement by April 4.

I would be happy to arrange a complimentary tasting session for you and your team. Please let me know a convenient date.

Best regards,
Daniel Reyes
