# Company Merger Announcement

PRESS RELEASE: STRATEGIC MERGER ANNOUNCEMENT

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE — March 22, 2026

Horizon Tech Solutions and CloudVista Inc. are pleased to announce that they have entered into a definitive merger agreement, creating one of the region's largest cloud technology providers. The combined entity, to be known as Horizon CloudVista, will serve over 2,000 enterprise clients across 14 countries.

Under the terms of the agreement, Horizon Tech will acquire all outstanding shares of CloudVista at $34 per share, representing a 22 percent premium over CloudVista's closing price on March 21. The transaction, valued at approximately $420 million, is expected to close in the third quarter of 2026, subject to regulatory approval and shareholder votes from both companies.

Katherine Reeves, CEO of Horizon Tech, will serve as CEO of the combined company, while Michael Santos, CEO of CloudVista, will assume the role of President and Chief Operating Officer. The company's headquarters will remain at Horizon Tech's current location in Seattle, with CloudVista's Denver office serving as a secondary hub.

No layoffs are anticipated as a result of the merger, as the companies' product lines are complementary rather than overlapping. However, some administrative functions may be consolidated over the next 18 months. The combined workforce will total approximately 3,400 employees.

Investor conference calls are scheduled for March 25 at 10:00 AM Eastern Time. Details are available at investor.horizontech.com.
