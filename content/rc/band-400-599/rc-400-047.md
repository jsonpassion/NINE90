# Company Wellness Initiative Results

WELLNESS INITIATIVE — YEAR-END RESULTS AND 2026 PLANS

To: All Employees
From: HR Wellness Committee
Date: March 21, 2026

We are pleased to share the results of our 2025 Employee Wellness Initiative, now entering its third year. Participation reached an all-time high of 68 percent of employees, up from 52 percent in 2024 and 35 percent in the program's inaugural year.

Key achievements in 2025 include: 340 employees completed the fitness challenge, collectively logging over 1.2 million minutes of exercise. The on-site health screening event in September was attended by 410 employees, with 73 percent following up with their healthcare providers based on screening results. Mental health workshop attendance increased by 45 percent compared to the previous year. The company's average sick days per employee decreased from 6.2 to 4.8 days, a reduction the wellness committee attributes in part to increased program engagement.

For 2026, we are expanding the program with several new offerings: a subsidized gym membership program covering 50 percent of fees up to $40 per month at participating fitness centers, quarterly nutrition workshops led by a registered dietitian, an expanded Employee Assistance Program with increased counseling sessions from 6 to 10 per year, and a new step-counting challenge with prizes for the top three departments each quarter.

Enrollment for the 2026 program opens April 1 on the HR portal. We encourage all employees to participate and take advantage of these resources designed to support your physical and mental well-being.
