# Workplace Safety Inspection

MEMORANDUM

To: All Floor Wardens and Safety Officers
From: Environmental Health & Safety Department
Date: March 24, 2026
Subject: Quarterly Safety Inspection — April 2026

The quarterly workplace safety inspection will be conducted from April 14 to April 18. Inspectors from SafeWork Consulting will visit all floors and facilities during regular business hours. This notice outlines what departments should prepare.

Before the inspection, each floor warden must verify that all fire extinguishers are accessible and have not passed their expiration date. Emergency exit paths must be clear of boxes, furniture, or other obstructions. First aid kits should be fully stocked according to the checklist posted inside each kit.

During the inspection, SafeWork consultants may interview employees about their knowledge of emergency procedures. All employees should be able to identify the location of the nearest fire exit and assembly point. A refresher email with this information will be sent to all staff on April 10.

Departments that receive a satisfactory rating will be recognized in the company newsletter. Departments with findings will be given 30 days to implement corrective actions, followed by a re-inspection at no additional cost.

Please distribute this information to your teams. A pre-inspection briefing for floor wardens will be held on April 9 at 10:00 AM in Meeting Room B. Attendance is mandatory.
