# Building Renovation and Temporary Arrangements

NOTICE: BUILDING B RENOVATION — TEMPORARY ARRANGEMENTS

Crestfield Industries is undertaking a comprehensive renovation of Building B to modernize the workspace and improve energy efficiency. Construction is scheduled from April 14 through June 30, 2026.

During the renovation period, the 120 employees currently based in Building B will be temporarily relocated as follows: the Marketing and Communications teams (45 employees) will move to the third floor of Building A, which has been reconfigured to accommodate them. The Product Development team (35 employees) will work from the newly leased temporary office at 200 Commerce Street, approximately one mile from campus. The remaining 40 employees in Administration and Finance will transition to a remote work arrangement for the duration of the project.

Key logistics: All Building B employees must clear their desks and personal items by April 11. IT will transfer computer equipment and phone lines to the temporary locations during the weekend of April 12-13. Mail and deliveries addressed to Building B will be automatically redirected to the Building A mailroom.

The renovation will include new HVAC systems, LED lighting throughout, modernized conference rooms with updated technology, and a redesigned open-plan layout with quiet focus rooms. The project budget is $2.4 million, and the company expects annual energy savings of approximately $85,000 once the upgrades are complete.

Updates will be posted monthly on the company intranet. Direct questions to facilities@crestfield.com.
