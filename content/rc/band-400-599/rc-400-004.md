# Office Renovation Schedule

To: All Employees — Building A
From: Facilities Management
Date: October 2
Subject: Office Renovation Schedule

Please be advised that Building A will undergo renovations beginning Monday, October 16, and continuing through Friday, November 10. The renovations will include updating the lighting systems, replacing carpeting, and repainting all common areas.

The work will be carried out in phases to minimize disruption to daily operations. The schedule is as follows:

Phase 1 (October 16–27): Third floor — All employees on the third floor will be temporarily relocated to the second floor of Building C. Temporary workstations have been set up in rooms C-201 through C-210.

Phase 2 (October 30–November 3): Second floor — Second-floor employees will move to the first floor of Building C, rooms C-101 through C-108.

Phase 3 (November 6–10): Lobby and first floor — First-floor employees will work from Building C, rooms C-301 through C-305. The main entrance will be closed during this phase, so please use the side entrance on Parker Street.

Employees should remove all personal items from their desks before their scheduled relocation date. IT support will be available to assist with the transfer of computers and equipment. For questions or concerns, please contact the Facilities Management team at ext. 450 or email facilities@company.com.

- Answer: C
