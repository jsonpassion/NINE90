# Operations Analyst Position

POSITION OPEN: OPERATIONS ANALYST
Summit Logistics Group — Dallas, TX

Summit Logistics Group, a national freight and distribution company handling over 50,000 shipments per month, is seeking an Operations Analyst to optimize our warehouse and transportation processes.

The Operations Analyst will analyze shipment data to identify inefficiencies and cost-saving opportunities, develop dashboards and reports using Power BI or Tableau for the operations leadership team, collaborate with warehouse managers to implement process improvements, model scenarios for route optimization and fleet utilization, and support the quarterly business review process with data-driven insights.

Qualifications: Bachelor's degree in operations management, supply chain, data analytics, or a related field. At least 3 years of experience in operations analysis or a similar role, preferably in logistics or distribution. Advanced Excel skills and proficiency in at least one data visualization tool are required. SQL experience is a strong plus. Candidates must be comfortable presenting findings to senior leadership.

We offer a salary of $68,000 to $82,000, annual performance bonuses, comprehensive health benefits, a 401(k) with 5 percent employer match, and tuition reimbursement up to $4,000 per year. This position is primarily on-site, with the option for one remote day per week.

Submit your resume and a sample analysis report or dashboard screenshot to careers@summitlogistics.com by April 14, 2026.
