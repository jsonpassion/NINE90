# Supply Chain Manager Position

POSITION AVAILABLE: SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGER
Bridgeport Manufacturing — Charlotte, NC

Bridgeport Manufacturing, a mid-size producer of industrial components with annual revenue of $85 million, seeks an experienced Supply Chain Manager to lead procurement, logistics, and inventory management operations.

Reporting to the Chief Operations Officer, the Supply Chain Manager will oversee a team of 18 professionals across purchasing, warehousing, and distribution. Key responsibilities include developing and executing sourcing strategies to reduce material costs by at least 5 percent annually, managing relationships with over 120 domestic and international suppliers, implementing lean inventory practices to reduce carrying costs while maintaining a 98 percent order fulfillment rate, negotiating freight contracts and optimizing transportation routes, and leading the transition to a new warehouse management system scheduled for Q3 2026.

Required qualifications include a bachelor's degree in supply chain management, business, or engineering, along with 8 or more years of supply chain experience with at least 4 years in a management role. APICS CSCP or CPSM certification is strongly preferred. The ideal candidate will have experience in manufacturing environments, strong negotiation skills, and proficiency with ERP systems (SAP experience is a plus).

Benefits include a salary range of $95,000 to $120,000, annual performance bonus of up to 20 percent, company car or $600 monthly car allowance, comprehensive benefits including health, dental, vision, and life insurance, 4 weeks PTO from the first year, and profit-sharing contributions to the retirement plan.

Apply at careers.bridgeportmfg.com or email your resume to talent@bridgeportmfg.com by April 18, 2026.

## Q1. What is the minimum cost reduction target for materials?
- A. 3 percent annually
- B. 10 percent annually
- C. 8 percent annually
- D. 5 percent annually
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The manager is expected to reduce material costs by at least 5 percent annually.

## Q2. What is the target order fulfillment rate?
- A. 95 percent
- B. 98 percent
- C. 96 percent
- D. 99.5 percent
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The goal is maintaining a 98 percent order fulfillment rate.

## Q3. What is the maximum annual performance bonus?
- A. 10 percent
- B. 15 percent
- C. 20 percent
- D. 25 percent
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Annual performance bonus of up to 20 percent.
