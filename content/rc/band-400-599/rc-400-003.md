# Employee Training Program

To: All Staff
From: Professional Development Office
Date: August 12
Subject: Fall Training Program Registration

The Professional Development Office is pleased to announce the fall training program schedule. Registration is now open and will close on August 31.

This quarter, we are offering the following courses:

Effective Business Writing — This two-day workshop covers email etiquette, report formatting, and persuasive writing techniques. Sessions will be held on September 5 and 6 in Conference Room B.

Project Management Fundamentals — A three-day course designed for employees who are new to project management roles. Topics include scheduling, budgeting, and risk assessment. The course will take place September 12 through 14 in the Training Center.

Customer Service Excellence — This one-day seminar focuses on communication skills, handling complaints, and building customer loyalty. It will be held on September 20 in the Main Auditorium.

All courses are free of charge for full-time employees. Part-time employees may enroll with their supervisor's approval. To register, visit the company intranet and select "Training Programs" from the menu, or contact the Professional Development Office at ext. 310.
