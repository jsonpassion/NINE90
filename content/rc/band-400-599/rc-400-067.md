# Employee Wellness Program

MEMORANDUM

To: All Employees
From: Human Resources
Date: March 23, 2026
Subject: Launch of Employee Wellness Program

The company is pleased to introduce a comprehensive employee wellness program beginning April 1, 2026. The program is designed to promote physical and mental health among our staff and is available to all full-time and part-time employees at no additional cost.

The program includes three main components. First, all employees will receive a complimentary membership to FitLife Gym, which has locations within walking distance of both our downtown and suburban offices. Second, we are partnering with MindWell Counseling to provide confidential mental health support. Employees may schedule up to six free counseling sessions per year through the MindWell app. Third, a monthly wellness workshop will be held in the main conference room, covering topics such as nutrition, stress management, and sleep hygiene.

Employees who participate in at least three wellness activities per quarter will be eligible for a $100 bonus added to their end-of-quarter paycheck. Activities include gym visits, counseling sessions, and workshop attendance. Participation is tracked through the new WellConnect app, which will be available for download on April 1.

Please review the detailed program guide attached to this memo and contact the HR department at wellness@company.com with any questions.

## Q1. Who is eligible for the wellness program?
- A. Only full-time employees
- B. Only managers and directors
- C. All full-time and part-time employees
- D. Employees with more than one year of service
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo states the program 'is available to all full-time and part-time employees.'

## Q3. What must employees do to earn the quarterly bonus?
- A. Complete an online health assessment
- B. Participate in at least three wellness activities per quarter
- C. Attend every monthly workshop
- D. Visit the gym at least 12 times per quarter
