# Product Launch Announcement

From: Marketing Department
To: All Regional Sales Managers
Date: March 21, 2026
Subject: Launch of ProClean 3000 — Sales Kit and Pricing

Dear Sales Team,

We are excited to announce the launch of the ProClean 3000, our newest commercial cleaning system, on April 15. This product replaces the ProClean 2500 and offers significant improvements, including a 30 percent increase in cleaning efficiency, a quieter motor, and a redesigned handle for better ergonomics.

The suggested retail price for the ProClean 3000 is $1,299. Customers who trade in a ProClean 2500 or competing brand model will receive a $200 discount. Volume orders of 10 or more units qualify for an additional 10 percent reduction. These promotional offers are valid through June 30.

Sales kits containing product specifications, comparison charts, and customer testimonials are being shipped to all regional offices and should arrive by April 10. A one-hour product training webinar will be held on April 12 at 2:00 PM Eastern Time. Attendance is required for all sales representatives. The recording will be available on the intranet for those who cannot attend live.

Please ensure your teams are prepared for the launch. Direct any product questions to the Product Development team at product@proclean.com.

## Q1. What improvement does the ProClean 3000 offer?
- A. It is 30 percent lighter.
- B. It has a 30 percent increase in cleaning efficiency.
- C. It costs 30 percent less than the previous model.
- D. It uses 30 percent less electricity.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email states the product offers 'a 30 percent increase in cleaning efficiency.'

## Q2. How much would a customer pay when trading in an old model?
- A. $999
- B. $1,049
- C. $1,099
- D. $1,199
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The retail price is $1,299 minus the $200 trade-in discount equals $1,099.

## Q3. When is the product training webinar?
- A. April 10
- B. April 12
- C. April 15
- D. June 30
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email says 'A one-hour product training webinar will be held on April 12.'
