# Annual Employee Recognition Awards

ANNUAL EMPLOYEE RECOGNITION AWARDS — CALL FOR NOMINATIONS

The Human Resources Department is pleased to announce that nominations are now open for the 2026 Annual Employee Recognition Awards. This program celebrates outstanding contributions and dedication across all departments.

Award categories this year include: Employee of the Year, Innovation Award, Customer Service Excellence, Team Collaboration Award, and the newly introduced Sustainability Champion Award for employees who have made significant contributions to our environmental initiatives.

Any employee may nominate a colleague by completing the nomination form available on the HR portal. Self-nominations are not accepted. Each nomination must include a written statement of no more than 500 words explaining why the nominee deserves the award, along with specific examples of their achievements. Nominations from managers carry additional weight but are not required.

The nomination deadline is April 30, 2026. A selection committee comprising senior leadership and past award winners will review all submissions and announce the winners at the Annual Gala Dinner on June 14. Each award winner will receive a crystal trophy, a $1,000 bonus, and two additional vacation days.

For questions about the nomination process, please contact the HR Awards Committee at awards@company.com.

## Q1. Which award category is new this year?
- A. Employee of the Year
- B. Innovation Award
- C. Customer Service Excellence
- D. Sustainability Champion Award
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The Sustainability Champion Award is described as newly introduced this year.

## Q2. Who is NOT eligible to nominate?

- C. Department managers
- D. Past award winners
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The announcement says self-nominations are not accepted.
