# Parking Policy Update

MEMORANDUM

To: All Employees
From: Administration Office
Date: March 23, 2026
Subject: Updated Parking Policy — Effective April 1

Effective April 1, 2026, the company will implement changes to the employee parking policy. These changes are necessary due to the construction of the new annex building, which will temporarily reduce the number of available spaces in Lot B.

Lot A will continue to be reserved for visitors and clients. Lot B will be reduced from 200 spaces to 120 spaces during construction, which is expected to last until September. Lot C, located two blocks east of the main building, has been leased to provide additional parking. A free shuttle bus will operate between Lot C and the main entrance every 15 minutes from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM.

Employees who currently have Lot B parking passes will retain their spaces. Employees on the waiting list will be assigned spaces in Lot C. Monthly parking fees for Lot C will be $60, compared to $85 for Lot B. Employees who choose to use Lot C will receive a $25 monthly transportation allowance to offset the inconvenience.

Carpooling groups of three or more employees will be given priority for remaining Lot B spaces. To form or join a carpool group, please use the new CarPool Connect feature on the company intranet.

Questions should be directed to the Administration Office at extension 2200.

## Q1. Why is the parking policy changing?
- A. The company is reducing parking costs.
- B. Construction of a new building is reducing available spaces.
- C. The city requires new parking regulations.
- D. Too many employees are driving to work.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo states the changes are 'due to the construction of the new annex building, which will temporarily reduce the number of available spaces in Lot B.'

## Q2. How often does the shuttle bus run between Lot C and the main building?
- A. Every 10 minutes
- B. Every 15 minutes
- C. Every 20 minutes
- D. Every 30 minutes
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The memo says the shuttle 'will operate between Lot C and the main entrance every 15 minutes.'

## Q3. What benefit do Lot C users receive?
- A. Free coffee vouchers
- B. Reserved spaces in Lot A
- C. A $25 monthly transportation allowance
- D. A reduced work schedule
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The memo states 'Employees who choose to use Lot C will receive a $25 monthly transportation allowance.'
