# Employee Travel Expense Policy

MEMORANDUM: UPDATED TRAVEL EXPENSE POLICY

To: All Employees
From: Finance Department
Date: March 19, 2026
Subject: Revised Travel and Expense Reimbursement Guidelines

Effective April 1, 2026, the following updated guidelines will govern all business travel expense reimbursements. Please review these changes carefully, as claims submitted under the old policy after March 31 will not be processed.

Airfare must be booked at economy class for domestic flights. Business class is permitted for international flights exceeding 8 hours. Hotel accommodations should not exceed $180 per night for domestic travel and $250 per night for international destinations. Meal expenses will be reimbursed up to $60 per day domestically and $85 per day internationally. Receipts are required for all individual expenses over $25.

All travel must be pre-approved by your department manager using the Travel Request Form on the company intranet. Expense reports must be submitted within 14 days of returning from the trip. Late submissions will be processed in the following month's reimbursement cycle.

The company will also now reimburse economy rideshare services (such as taxi or app-based services) between airports and hotels. Personal vehicle mileage is reimbursed at $0.58 per mile. For questions, contact the Finance Department at travel@company.com.

## Q1. When is business class airfare permitted?
- A. For all international flights
- B. Only with executive approval
- C. For flights over 4 hours
- D. For international flights exceeding 8 hours
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Business class is permitted for international flights exceeding 8 hours.

## Q2. What is the daily meal reimbursement limit for international travel?
- A. $50
- B. $60
- C. $75
- D. $85
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Meal expenses are reimbursed up to $85 per day internationally.

## Q3. How soon must expense reports be submitted after a trip?
- A. 14 days
- B. 10 days
- C. 7 days
- D. 30 days
- Answer: A
- Explanation: Expense reports must be submitted within 14 days of returning from the trip.
