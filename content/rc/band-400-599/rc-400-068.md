# Vendor Selection Notice

From: Jennifer Walsh, Procurement Manager
To: Department Heads
Date: March 21, 2026
Subject: Office Furniture Vendor Selection — Input Requested

Dear Department Heads,

As part of our office modernization initiative, we are in the process of selecting a new office furniture vendor. Our current contract with Morrison Office Solutions expires on May 31, and we have received proposals from three vendors. I am writing to request your input before we make a final decision.

The three finalists are as follows. Vertex Workspace has proposed a full office setup at $340 per workstation, with a five-year warranty and free installation. BrightSpace Design has offered a more customizable option at $410 per workstation, with a three-year warranty and a dedicated design consultant. Nova Furnishings has submitted the most affordable proposal at $285 per workstation, but with only a two-year warranty and an additional $40 per unit installation charge.

We plan to outfit approximately 150 workstations across three floors. The total budget approved for this project is $55,000. Delivery and installation must be completed by July 15 to align with the renovation schedule.

Please review the detailed proposals in the shared drive under Procurement > Furniture RFP 2026 and submit your preferences along with any specific requirements by April 4. A final selection meeting will be held on April 10.

Your feedback is valued and will be taken into consideration. Thank you.

## Q1. Why is the company selecting a new furniture vendor?
- A. The current vendor went out of business.
- B. The current contract is expiring on May 31.
- C. The current furniture is being recalled.
- D. Employees complained about the existing furniture.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email states 'Our current contract with Morrison Office Solutions expires on May 31.'

## Q2. Which vendor offers the lowest total cost per workstation including installation?
- A. Vertex Workspace
- B. BrightSpace Design
- C. Nova Furnishings
- D. Morrison Office Solutions
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Nova charges $285 plus $40 installation = $325 per unit, which is lower than Vertex at $340 and BrightSpace at $410.

## Q3. When must feedback be submitted?
- A. March 31
- B. April 4
- C. April 10
- D. May 31
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email says 'submit your preferences along with any specific requirements by April 4.'
