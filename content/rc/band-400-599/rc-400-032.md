# New Employee Benefits Portal

ANNOUNCEMENT: LAUNCH OF NEW EMPLOYEE BENEFITS PORTAL

Effective April 7, 2026, Pacific Dynamics is launching a redesigned Employee Benefits Portal to replace the current system. The new portal, accessible at benefits.pacificdynamics.com, offers a streamlined interface that makes it easier to manage your health insurance, retirement accounts, paid time off, and other benefits.

Key features of the new portal include a personalized dashboard showing your current benefit selections and usage, the ability to compare health plan options side by side, direct access to your 401(k) account balance and investment allocations, a mobile-friendly design that works on smartphones and tablets, and real-time tracking of your remaining PTO balance.

All employees must log in to the new portal between April 7 and April 21 to verify that their personal information and beneficiary designations are correct. During this review period, you may also make changes to your health plan selection without waiting for the annual open enrollment period in November.

Training sessions on the new portal will be held in the main conference room on April 3 at 10:00 AM and April 4 at 2:00 PM. Each session will last approximately 45 minutes and will cover navigation, common tasks, and troubleshooting. Attendance is recommended but not mandatory, as video tutorials will also be available on the portal.

For technical assistance, contact the Benefits Help Desk at ext. 5500 or benefits-help@pacificdynamics.com.

## Q1. What must employees do between April 7 and April 21?
- A. Verify personal information and beneficiary designations
- B. Complete a benefits survey
- C. Select new health insurance plans
- D. Attend a mandatory training session
- Answer: A
- Explanation: All employees must verify their personal information and beneficiary designations during this period.

## Q2. What special opportunity is available during the review period?
- A. Employees can increase their 401(k) contributions
- B. Employees can negotiate salary adjustments
- C. Employees can request additional PTO days
- D. Employees can change health plans outside open enrollment
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Employees may make changes to their health plan selection without waiting for open enrollment.

## Q3. How long are the training sessions?
- A. 30 minutes
- B. 60 minutes
- C. 45 minutes
- D. 90 minutes
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Each session will last approximately 45 minutes.
