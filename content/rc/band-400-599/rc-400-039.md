# Digital Marketing Coordinator

HIRING: DIGITAL MARKETING COORDINATOR
Skyline Media Group — San Francisco, CA

Skyline Media Group, a fast-growing digital marketing agency, is seeking a Digital Marketing Coordinator to manage social media campaigns, email marketing, and content creation for our diverse portfolio of clients.

Primary responsibilities: Plan and schedule content across social media platforms (Instagram, LinkedIn, Facebook, and X) for 8 to 10 client accounts, create and manage email marketing campaigns using Mailchimp or similar platforms, track campaign performance using Google Analytics and prepare weekly reports, coordinate with the design team to produce visual content, and assist in the development of content calendars and marketing strategies.

Requirements: Bachelor's degree in marketing, communications, or equivalent experience, 2 to 4 years of experience in digital marketing or social media management, demonstrated proficiency with social media scheduling tools and analytics platforms, strong writing and editing skills, and the ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously with attention to detail.

Compensation and benefits: Salary range of $52,000 to $62,000, quarterly performance bonuses, health and vision insurance, flexible work hours with the option to work remotely on Fridays, 18 days of PTO plus company holidays, and access to online learning platforms for professional development.

To apply, send your resume, a brief cover letter, and links to two social media accounts you have managed to hiring@skylinemedia.com. Priority consideration for applications received by April 10, 2026.
