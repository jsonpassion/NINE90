# New Corporate Sustainability Report

ANNOUNCEMENT: 2025 CORPORATE SUSTAINABILITY REPORT NOW AVAILABLE

Meridian Holdings is proud to publish its fifth annual Corporate Sustainability Report, detailing our environmental, social, and governance (ESG) performance for the 2025 fiscal year. The full report is available for download on our website at meridianholdings.com/sustainability.

Environmental highlights: We reduced total carbon emissions by 23 percent compared to 2024, exceeding our target of 15 percent. Water consumption decreased by 11 percent through the installation of recycling systems at our three manufacturing plants. Additionally, 92 percent of manufacturing waste was diverted from landfills through recycling and composting programs.

Social impact: The Meridian Foundation donated $2.3 million to community programs focused on education and workforce development. Employee volunteer hours totaled 12,400, with 340 employees participating in organized community projects. Our employee satisfaction score rose to 4.2 out of 5.0, the highest in company history.

Governance: The board of directors increased its diversity, with women now representing 40 percent of board members, up from 33 percent in 2024. We also implemented a new whistleblower protection policy and enhanced our anti-corruption training program.

Looking ahead to 2026, our goals include achieving carbon neutrality in office operations by December, increasing supplier sustainability audits by 50 percent, and launching an employee green commuting incentive program. We invite all stakeholders to review the report and share their feedback at sustainability@meridian.com.

- Explanation: Total carbon emissions were reduced by 23 percent compared to 2024.

- B. Install solar panels at all locations
