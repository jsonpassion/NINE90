# Q2 Budget Allocation Update

To: Department Heads
From: CFO Office — Linda Torres
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: Second Quarter Budget Allocation

Following last week's financial review, I am pleased to share the budget allocation for Q2 2026. The total operating budget for the quarter has been set at $2.4 million, representing a 10 percent increase compared to Q1.

The marketing department will receive $600,000, the largest share, to support the upcoming product launch campaign. Research and development has been allocated $500,000 to continue work on our next-generation product line. Operations will receive $450,000, and human resources has been assigned $350,000 to fund the new employee wellness program and recruitment efforts. The remaining $500,000 will be distributed among IT, legal, and administrative departments.

Please note that all budget requests exceeding $10,000 must be approved by the finance team before any purchase orders are issued. Department heads are expected to submit monthly spending reports by the fifth business day of each month.

Any department that expects to exceed its quarterly allocation should notify the CFO office at least two weeks in advance to discuss possible adjustments. Budget transfers between departments require written approval from both department heads and the CFO.

- Answer: C
