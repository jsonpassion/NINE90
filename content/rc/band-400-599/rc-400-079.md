# Warehouse Relocation

MEMORANDUM

To: Warehouse Operations Team
From: Logistics Director, Mark Sullivan
Date: March 23, 2026
Subject: Warehouse Relocation — Timeline and Responsibilities

As previously announced, our warehouse operations will be relocating from the current facility on Industrial Road to the new distribution center at 450 Commerce Boulevard. The new facility offers 40 percent more storage capacity, modern loading docks, and an automated inventory tracking system.

The relocation will occur over three weekends to minimize disruption to daily operations. Weekend 1 (April 12-13): Non-essential inventory and archived materials will be moved. Weekend 2 (April 19-20): Active inventory for products A through M will be transferred. Weekend 3 (April 26-27): Remaining active inventory, equipment, and office furniture will complete the move.

All warehouse staff are expected to work on their assigned relocation weekend. Overtime compensation at 1.5 times the regular hourly rate will be paid for weekend hours. Lunch will be provided on all relocation days.

The new facility will require all forklift operators to complete a safety orientation specific to the new layout. Orientations are scheduled for April 8 and April 9. Operators must attend one of these sessions before working at the new site.

Regular operations at the new facility will begin on Monday, April 28. The old facility will be cleaned and returned to the property owner by May 15.

Contact me directly at mark.sullivan@company.com with any concerns.
