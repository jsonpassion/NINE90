# Trade Show Follow-Up Strategy

From: Lisa Chang, VP of Sales
To: Sales Team
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: Post-Trade Show Lead Follow-Up Protocol

Dear Team,

Our booth at the InnovateTech Expo last week generated an impressive 412 leads, exceeding our target of 350 by 18 percent. To capitalize on this momentum, I am implementing a structured follow-up protocol that must begin immediately.

Leads have been categorized into three tiers based on qualification criteria. Tier 1 (87 leads) consists of prospects who requested a product demonstration or pricing information — these must receive a personalized email and phone call within 48 hours. Tier 2 (156 leads) includes attendees who expressed general interest and spent significant time at our booth — they should receive a follow-up email with a product brochure within 5 business days. Tier 3 (169 leads) covers those who simply scanned their badge or took a brochure — they will be added to our newsletter list and receive a drip email campaign.

Each sales representative has been assigned leads based on geographic territory. You will find your assigned leads in the CRM system under the "InnovateTech 2026" campaign. For Tier 1 leads, please log all contact attempts and outcomes in the CRM. I will review the Tier 1 follow-up report on April 4 to assess conversion progress.

Our target is to convert at least 15 percent of Tier 1 leads into qualified opportunities within 30 days. Representatives who achieve the highest conversion rates will be recognized at the May sales meeting.

Please contact me directly if you have questions about any specific lead assignments.
