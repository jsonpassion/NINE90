# Customer Satisfaction Survey Results

To: Senior Leadership Team
From: Priya Sharma, Customer Experience Manager
Date: March 21, 2026
Subject: Annual Customer Satisfaction Survey — Key Findings

I am pleased to present the highlights of our 2025 Annual Customer Satisfaction Survey. We received 3,840 responses, representing a response rate of 32 percent — up from 24 percent the previous year.

Overall customer satisfaction scored 4.1 out of 5.0, a modest improvement from 3.9 in the prior year. The areas receiving the highest ratings were product quality (4.4) and the ordering process (4.3). The area with the most room for improvement remains after-sales support, which received a score of 3.2, though this is up from 2.8 last year.

Among the most frequently cited concerns, 43 percent of respondents mentioned long wait times for phone support, 31 percent noted difficulty navigating the website for product information, and 22 percent expressed a desire for more flexible return policies. On the positive side, 78 percent of respondents said they would recommend our products to others, and 65 percent indicated they plan to increase their purchases in the coming year.

Based on these findings, I recommend three priority actions: expanding the phone support team by four additional representatives to reduce wait times, redesigning the product information section of the website by Q3, and extending the return window from 30 to 45 days as a trial for the remainder of 2026.

I am happy to present the full report at next week's leadership meeting.
