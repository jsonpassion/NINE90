# HR Business Partner

CAREER OPPORTUNITY: HR BUSINESS PARTNER
Coastal Dynamics Corporation — Miami, FL

Coastal Dynamics Corporation, a marine engineering firm with 450 employees across three offices, is seeking an HR Business Partner to support our Engineering and Project Management divisions (approximately 200 employees).

The HR Business Partner will serve as the primary HR contact for division leaders, advising on talent management, organizational design, and employee relations. Specific duties include partnering with directors to develop workforce planning strategies, managing the full employee lifecycle from recruitment through offboarding, leading performance review processes and calibration sessions, analyzing HR metrics including turnover, engagement scores, and time-to-fill data, identifying training needs and coordinating with the Learning and Development team, and managing employee relations issues including investigations when necessary.

Required qualifications: Bachelor's degree in human resources, business, or a related discipline. A minimum of 6 years of progressive HR experience, including at least 2 years as an HR business partner or equivalent strategic HR role. PHR or SHRM-CP certification is required; SPHR or SHRM-SCP is preferred. Strong knowledge of employment law and experience with HRIS platforms.

Compensation: Salary range of $85,000 to $105,000 based on qualifications, annual performance bonus, comprehensive medical, dental, and vision insurance, company-funded pension plan contributing 8 percent of salary, 22 days PTO plus 10 company holidays, and relocation assistance negotiable.

Submit applications to hr-careers@coastaldynamics.com by April 11, 2026. Include your resume, cover letter, and the names of three professional references.
