# Environmental Compliance Specialist

CAREER OPPORTUNITY: ENVIRONMENTAL COMPLIANCE SPECIALIST
Meridian Manufacturing — Austin, TX

Meridian Manufacturing, a leader in sustainable industrial products, is seeking an Environmental Compliance Specialist to ensure our operations meet all federal, state, and local environmental regulations. This newly created position reflects our commitment to expanding our sustainability programs.

The successful candidate will be responsible for monitoring and maintaining compliance with EPA regulations and state environmental laws, conducting regular environmental audits of our three manufacturing facilities, preparing and submitting required environmental reports to regulatory agencies, developing waste reduction strategies and tracking their implementation, and training plant managers and supervisors on environmental compliance requirements.

Required qualifications include a bachelor's degree in environmental science, environmental engineering, or a related field, a minimum of 4 years of experience in environmental compliance preferably in manufacturing, familiarity with Clean Air Act and Clean Water Act regulations, and strong analytical and report-writing skills. A Certified Environmental Professional (CEP) designation is preferred but not required.

Compensation: $72,000 to $88,000 base salary, annual bonus potential of up to 10 percent, comprehensive benefits including medical, dental, and vision insurance, 15 days PTO increasing to 20 days after 3 years, and relocation assistance of up to $5,000 for out-of-state candidates.

Apply by submitting your resume and cover letter to careers@meridianmfg.com. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis, with priority given to submissions received by April 18, 2026.
