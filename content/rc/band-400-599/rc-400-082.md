# New Software Improves Customer Service Response Time

DataFlow Solutions announced on Tuesday the launch of its new customer service management software, QuickReply 3.0. The software is designed to help businesses respond to customer inquiries up to 40 percent faster than traditional email-based systems.

QuickReply 3.0 uses an intelligent routing system that automatically directs customer messages to the most appropriate support agent based on the topic and urgency of the inquiry. The software also includes a built-in knowledge base that suggests answers to frequently asked questions, reducing the time agents spend searching for information.

The software is available in three subscription plans: Basic at $29 per month, Professional at $79 per month, and Enterprise at $149 per month. The Basic plan supports up to five agents, while the Professional plan allows up to 20 agents. The Enterprise plan has no agent limit and includes additional analytics tools.

Companies interested in trying the software can sign up for a free 30-day trial on the DataFlow Solutions website at www.dataflowsolutions.com.
