# Airport Expansion Project to Add New Terminal

Regional Airport Authority officials announced the approval of a $320 million expansion project that will add a new domestic terminal to Westfield International Airport. The project is scheduled to break ground in January and is expected to take three years to complete.

The new Terminal C will feature 18 gates, a modern food court with 12 dining options, and a business lounge with complimentary Wi-Fi and charging stations. The terminal will also include automated check-in kiosks and advanced baggage handling systems designed to reduce wait times by up to 25 percent.

The expansion is being driven by a 35 percent increase in passenger traffic over the past five years. Airport officials estimate that the new terminal will accommodate an additional 5 million passengers annually, bringing the airport's total capacity to 18 million.

During construction, travelers may experience temporary changes to parking areas and shuttle routes. The airport has set up a dedicated information page at www.westfieldairport.com/expansion for updates and travel advisories.
