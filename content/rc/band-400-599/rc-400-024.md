# Senior Graphic Designer Position

POSITION AVAILABLE: SENIOR GRAPHIC DESIGNER
Company: BrightWave Marketing Agency
Location: 320 Harbor Boulevard, Suite 210, Portland
Employment Type: Full-time

BrightWave Marketing Agency is seeking a Senior Graphic Designer to join our creative team. The ideal candidate will have at least 5 years of professional design experience and expertise in Adobe Creative Suite, particularly Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. Experience with motion graphics and video editing is a strong advantage but not required.

Responsibilities include leading design projects from concept to completion, collaborating with copywriters and account managers on client campaigns, maintaining brand consistency across all materials, and mentoring junior designers on the team.

We offer a competitive salary range of $65,000 to $85,000 depending on experience, comprehensive health and dental insurance, 20 days of paid time off, and a professional development budget of $2,000 per year. Remote work is available two days per week after the initial 90-day onboarding period.

To apply, submit your resume, portfolio, and a cover letter explaining why you would be a great fit for our team. Applications should be sent to careers@brightwave.com by April 15, 2026. Interviews will be conducted during the last two weeks of April.
