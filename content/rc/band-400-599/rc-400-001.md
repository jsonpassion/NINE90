# Annual Company Picnic

To: All Employees
From: Human Resources Department
Date: June 3
Subject: Annual Company Picnic

We are pleased to announce that this year's annual company picnic will be held on Saturday, June 17, from 11:00 A.M. to 4:00 P.M. at Riverside Park. All employees and their families are welcome to attend.

A variety of food and beverages will be provided, including grilled items, salads, and desserts. Vegetarian options will also be available. Please inform the HR department of any dietary restrictions by June 10 so that we can make the necessary arrangements.

Activities will include volleyball, a relay race, and a raffle drawing with prizes such as gift cards and electronics. Employees who wish to volunteer to help organize activities should contact Sarah Mitchell at ext. 204.

Free parking is available at the park. For those who prefer public transportation, Bus Route 15 stops directly in front of the park entrance. We look forward to seeing everyone there.
