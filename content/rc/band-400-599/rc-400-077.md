# Remote Work Guidelines

From: Human Resources
To: All Employees
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: Updated Remote Work Guidelines — Effective April 1

Dear Employees,

Based on feedback from the recent employee survey, we are updating our remote work policy to provide greater flexibility while maintaining team collaboration. The following changes take effect on April 1.

Eligible employees may now work remotely up to three days per week, increased from the current two-day limit. All employees must be in the office on Tuesdays and Thursdays, which are designated as mandatory in-office days for team meetings and cross-departmental collaboration. The remaining three days — Monday, Wednesday, and Friday — are flexible, meaning employees may choose to work from home or the office.

To maintain productivity, remote employees must be available on company communication tools between 9:00 AM and 4:00 PM, which are designated as core hours. Employees must have a reliable internet connection and a quiet workspace. The company will provide a one-time $300 home office stipend to employees who have not previously received one.

Managers will review remote work arrangements quarterly to ensure they meet team and business needs. Employees in their first 90 days of employment are required to work in the office full-time to facilitate onboarding and training.

Please review the full policy document on the HR portal and speak with your manager about your schedule preferences.
