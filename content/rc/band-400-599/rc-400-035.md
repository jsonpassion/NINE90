# Professional Development Policy

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT REIMBURSEMENT POLICY

Effective Date: April 1, 2026
Applicable to: All full-time employees who have completed at least one year of service

Sterling Associates is committed to the professional growth of its employees. This policy outlines the guidelines for our enhanced Professional Development Reimbursement Program.

Each eligible employee may receive up to $3,500 per calendar year for approved professional development activities. Eligible expenses include tuition for job-related courses at accredited institutions, professional certification exams and study materials, industry conference registration fees, and professional association memberships.

To request reimbursement, employees must submit a Professional Development Request Form to their direct supervisor at least 30 days before the start of the activity. The supervisor must approve the request based on its relevance to the employee's current role or career development plan. Upon completion, the employee must submit receipts and proof of completion (such as a certificate or grade report showing a passing grade of C or higher) to the HR department within 45 days.

Reimbursement is processed through payroll and typically appears within two pay periods after approval. Employees who voluntarily leave the company within 12 months of receiving reimbursement must repay 50 percent of the amount received.

The company also offers up to 5 days of paid study leave per year for employees preparing for approved professional certification exams. Study leave must be requested at least two weeks in advance.

For more information, contact the Learning and Development team at development@sterling.com.
