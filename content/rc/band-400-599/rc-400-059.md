# Financial Controller Position

NOW HIRING: FINANCIAL CONTROLLER
Redwood Partners LLP — Boston, MA

Redwood Partners LLP, a professional services firm with $42 million in annual revenue and 180 employees, is seeking an experienced Financial Controller to lead our accounting operations and financial reporting functions.

Reporting to the Chief Financial Officer, the Financial Controller will oversee a team of 8 accounting professionals responsible for the full accounting cycle, including accounts payable, accounts receivable, general ledger, and payroll. The role requires hands-on involvement in month-end and year-end closing processes, which must be completed within 5 business days and 15 business days respectively.

Key responsibilities: Ensure accurate and timely financial reporting in accordance with US GAAP, manage the annual external audit process and serve as the primary liaison with auditors, prepare monthly financial analysis packages for the executive team and partners, oversee cash flow management and banking relationships, implement and maintain strong internal controls, lead the annual budgeting process in collaboration with department heads, and evaluate and improve financial systems and processes.

Qualifications: Bachelor's degree in accounting or finance (MBA preferred), CPA certification required, minimum 10 years of accounting experience with at least 5 years in a controller or senior financial management role, expertise in US GAAP and financial reporting, experience with NetSuite or similar ERP systems, and strong leadership and team development skills.

Compensation: Base salary of $130,000 to $160,000, annual performance bonus of 15 to 25 percent, partnership track eligibility after 3 years, comprehensive benefits package, and 4 weeks PTO.

Apply confidentially to recruiting@redwoodpartners.com by April 21, 2026.
