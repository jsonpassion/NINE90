# Small Business Loans Now Available at Lower Interest Rates

Crescent Bank has announced a new small business loan program with interest rates starting at 4.9 percent, significantly lower than the industry average of 7.2 percent. The program is designed to support local entrepreneurs looking to start or expand their businesses.

Loans range from $10,000 to $250,000 with repayment terms of up to seven years. Applicants must have been in operation for at least six months and demonstrate a clear business plan. Startups with less than six months of operation may qualify for a separate microloan program with amounts up to $25,000.

To simplify the application process, Crescent Bank has launched an online portal where business owners can submit applications, upload required documents, and track the status of their loan. Most applications are reviewed within five business days, and approved funds are typically disbursed within two weeks.

Business owners interested in learning more can attend a free workshop at the main branch on the first Saturday of every month. Walk-in consultations are also available during regular banking hours.
