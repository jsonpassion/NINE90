# Client Project Status Update

From: Thomas Wright, Project Manager
To: Sandra Kim, Client Relations Director
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: Parkview Hotel Renovation Project — Status Update

Dear Sandra,

Please find below the monthly status update for the Parkview Hotel renovation project, which we can share with the client at next week's meeting.

Project overview: The renovation of the 180-room Parkview Hotel is currently 62 percent complete, slightly ahead of the original schedule. The project started on November 1, 2025, with a target completion date of August 15, 2026. Our current projection indicates we may finish up to two weeks early.

Phase 1 (guest rooms, floors 1-4) is complete. All 92 rooms have been renovated with new furnishings, updated bathrooms, and smart room controls. Client inspection on March 15 resulted in a satisfaction score of 9.2 out of 10.

Phase 2 (guest rooms, floors 5-8) is 65 percent complete and on schedule for a May 30 completion. The remaining 88 rooms are progressing smoothly, with an average of 4 rooms being completed per week.

Phase 3 (lobby, restaurant, and conference center) has not yet begun but is scheduled to start June 2. We are currently finalizing material selections with the client's interior designer and expect all decisions to be confirmed by April 15.

Budget status: Total project budget is $4.8 million. Expenditures to date are $2.95 million, which is $40,000 under budget. However, we anticipate an additional cost of approximately $65,000 for upgraded lighting fixtures that the client requested for the lobby area during last month's review meeting.

Please let me know if you need additional details for the client presentation.
