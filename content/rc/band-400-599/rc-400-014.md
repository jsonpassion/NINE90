# Office Relocation Announcement

To: All Employees
From: Operations Department
Date: March 19, 2026
Subject: Office Relocation — Effective May 1

We are excited to announce that our company will be moving to a new office location effective May 1, 2026. The new address is 800 Commerce Boulevard, Suite 400, in the Eastgate Business District. This move will provide us with 40 percent more floor space to accommodate our growing team.

The new office features modern open-plan workspaces, six conference rooms equipped with the latest video conferencing technology, a cafeteria, a fitness center, and a rooftop terrace. Underground parking will be available for all employees at no charge, and the building is located within a five-minute walk of the Central Line subway station.

The moving process will take place over the weekend of April 26 and 27. Employees are asked to pack their personal belongings and label all boxes by end of day on Friday, April 25. Packing materials will be distributed starting April 14. IT equipment, including computers and monitors, will be handled by the IT department and does not need to be packed by employees.

An orientation tour of the new office will be offered on April 22 and 23. Please sign up through the intranet by April 18.

## Q1. How much additional floor space will the new office provide?
- A. 20 percent more
- B. 30 percent more
- C. 40 percent more
- D. 50 percent more
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The announcement states "this move will provide us with 40 percent more floor space to accommodate our growing team."

- Answer: B
- Explanation: The announcement states "IT equipment, including computers and monitors, will be handled by the IT department and does not need to be packed by employees."

## Q3. When is the deadline to sign up for the orientation tour?
- A. April 14
- B. April 18

- D. April 25
