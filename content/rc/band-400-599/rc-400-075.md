# Sustainability Initiative

MEMORANDUM

To: All Departments
From: Corporate Sustainability Office
Date: March 23, 2026
Subject: Green Office Initiative — Phase 2

Following the success of Phase 1 of our Green Office Initiative, which reduced paper consumption by 35 percent and energy costs by 18 percent over the past year, we are now launching Phase 2 with additional measures to further reduce our environmental impact.

Beginning April 1, single-use plastic cups and utensils will no longer be available in break rooms and cafeterias. Instead, each employee will receive a reusable water bottle and a set of bamboo utensils. Water refill stations are being installed on every floor and will be operational by March 31.

We are also introducing a composting program for food waste in the cafeteria. Clearly labeled composting bins will be placed next to existing recycling stations. The facilities team will provide a brief orientation during the first week of April.

Additionally, departments that reduce their printing volume by 20 percent compared to Q1 2026 will receive a $500 team activity fund at the end of Q2. Printing data will be tracked automatically through the existing printer management system.

Your participation is essential to meeting our goal of becoming carbon neutral by 2030. For suggestions or questions, please reach out to sustainability@company.com.
