# Professional Development Workshops

The Training and Development Department is offering a series of professional development workshops during the month of September. These workshops are free for all employees and are designed to enhance skills relevant to your career growth.

Available workshops:
- Effective Presentation Skills (September 5, 9:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.)
- Project Management Fundamentals (September 12, 9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.)
- Advanced Excel for Data Analysis (September 19, 1:00 p.m. - 5:00 p.m.)
- Conflict Resolution in the Workplace (September 26, 10:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.)

Each workshop has a maximum capacity of 25 participants. Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis through the learning management system on the company intranet. Employees who attend a workshop will receive a certificate of completion and may be eligible for professional development credits.

Managers are encouraged to identify workshops that align with their team members' development goals and discuss attendance during upcoming one-on-one meetings.

## Q1. Which workshop is a full-day session?
- A. Effective Presentation Skills
- B. Conflict Resolution in the Workplace
- C. Advanced Excel for Data Analysis
- D. Project Management Fundamentals
- Answer: D
- Explanation: Project Management Fundamentals runs from "9:00 a.m. - 4:00 p.m.," making it a full-day session.

## Q2. How are workshop spots allocated?
- A. By manager recommendation
- B. By seniority
- C. On a first-come, first-served basis
- D. By random selection
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says "Registration is on a first-come, first-served basis."

## Q3. What do attendees receive after completing a workshop?
- A. A cash bonus
- B. A certificate of completion
- C. A promotion opportunity
- D. Extra vacation days
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states "Employees who attend a workshop will receive a certificate of completion."
