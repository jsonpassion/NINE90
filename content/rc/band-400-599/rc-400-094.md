# New Supplier Agreement for Raw Materials

To: Production and Procurement Teams
From: James Morton, Supply Chain Director
Date: July 10

I would like to inform you that effective August 1, we will be transitioning to a new primary supplier for steel and aluminum components. After a thorough evaluation process, we have selected Atlas Metal Works, which offered competitive pricing and demonstrated strong quality assurance capabilities during the trial period.

The transition will be gradual. During August, we will receive shipments from both our current supplier, Pacific Materials, and Atlas Metal Works. By September 1, all orders will be placed exclusively with Atlas Metal Works. This approach ensures we maintain adequate inventory levels during the changeover.

Key benefits of the new agreement include a 12 percent cost reduction on steel components, faster delivery times of 5 business days instead of the current 8, and a guaranteed quality standard with a defect rate below 0.5 percent.

Team leaders should update their procurement forms with the new supplier codes, which will be distributed by email next week. For any supply-related concerns during the transition, contact the procurement office at extension 6200.
