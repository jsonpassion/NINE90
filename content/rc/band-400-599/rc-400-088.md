# Office Lease Renewal Notice

To: All Tenants of Clarkson Business Center
From: Building Management
Date: September 5

This letter serves as a reminder that the current lease agreements for all office spaces in Clarkson Business Center will expire on December 31. Tenants who wish to renew their leases for the upcoming year are asked to submit a written renewal request to the management office by October 31.

Please be aware that rental rates for the new term will increase by 4 percent, reflecting recent improvements to the building's common areas, including the renovated lobby, upgraded elevators, and new security system. The updated rate schedule is enclosed with this letter and is also available on the building's website.

Tenants who choose not to renew must vacate their spaces by December 31 and complete a move-out inspection with the maintenance staff. Security deposits will be refunded within 30 days of the inspection, provided the space is returned in satisfactory condition.

For questions about the renewal process or building improvements, please contact the management office at 555-0633 or visit us in Suite 101.

## Q1. When do current lease agreements expire?
- A. December 31
- B. October 31
- C. November 30
- D. September 30
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The notice says "current lease agreements for all office spaces will expire on December 31."

## Q2. Why are rental rates increasing?
- A. Due to inflation adjustments
- B. Because of building improvements
- C. To cover insurance costs
- D. Because of new government taxes
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice says the increase reflects "recent improvements to the building's common areas, including the renovated lobby, upgraded elevators, and new security system."

## Q3. When will security deposits be refunded?
- A. Immediately upon moving out
- B. Within 14 days of the inspection
- C. Within 30 days of the inspection
- D. At the end of the next quarter
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states "Security deposits will be refunded within 30 days of the inspection."
