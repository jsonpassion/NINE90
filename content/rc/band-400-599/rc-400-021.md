# Quarterly Sales Report Discussion

From: Robert Tan, Regional Sales Manager
To: All Sales Team Members
Date: March 20, 2026
Subject: Q1 Sales Performance Review Meeting

Dear Team,

I am writing to inform you that our Q1 sales performance review meeting has been scheduled for Friday, April 4 at 10:00 AM in Conference Room A. The meeting is expected to last approximately two hours, and attendance is mandatory for all regional sales representatives.

Our preliminary figures indicate that the Northern region exceeded its quarterly target by 12 percent, while the Southern and Western regions fell short by 5 percent and 8 percent respectively. Overall company revenue for Q1 stands at $4.2 million, which represents a 3 percent increase over the same period last year.

Please prepare a brief summary of your top three accounts and any challenges you faced this quarter. Each representative will have 10 minutes to present. Additionally, I would like each of you to propose at least one strategy for improving Q2 results. If you are unable to attend in person, please contact my assistant, Linda Park, at extension 4420 to arrange a video conference connection.

I look forward to a productive discussion.
