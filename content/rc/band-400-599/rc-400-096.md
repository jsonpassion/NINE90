# Product Recall Notice

Dear Customer,

BrightHome Appliances is voluntarily recalling its Model RX-200 Portable Space Heater due to a potential overheating issue identified during routine safety testing. Approximately 15,000 units sold between January and March of this year are affected.

While no injuries have been reported, we take customer safety very seriously. Customers who own a Model RX-200 should immediately stop using the heater and unplug it from the wall outlet. To check if your unit is affected, look for the serial number on the bottom of the heater. Serial numbers between RX200-10001 and RX200-25000 are included in this recall.

Affected customers have two options: a full refund of the purchase price or a free replacement with the updated Model RX-210, which has an improved safety mechanism. To begin the return process, call our recall hotline at 1-800-555-0340 or visit www.brighthome.com/recall.

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience and thank you for your understanding.

## Q1. Why is the product being recalled?
- A. It has a potential overheating issue
- B. The heater is too noisy
- C. The power cord is too short
- D. It does not heat effectively
- Answer: A
- Explanation: The notice says the recall is "due to a potential overheating issue identified during routine safety testing."

## Q2. How many units are affected by the recall?
- A. 5,000
- B. 10,000
- C. 15,000
- D. 25,000
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states "Approximately 15,000 units sold between January and March of this year are affected."

## Q3. What options are available to affected customers?
- A. A repair at no charge
- B. A store credit
- C. A refund or a free replacement
- D. A discount on a future purchase
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice says customers can choose "a full refund of the purchase price or a free replacement with the updated Model RX-210."
