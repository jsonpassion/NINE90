# Updated Travel Expense Policy

To: All Employees
From: Richard Yoon, Chief Financial Officer
Date: March 18

Please be advised that the company's travel expense policy has been updated, effective April 1. The changes are designed to streamline the reimbursement process while maintaining responsible spending practices.

Key changes include:
- The daily meal allowance has been increased from $50 to $65 for domestic travel and from $75 to $90 for international travel.
- Hotel expenses must not exceed $180 per night for domestic travel. Exceptions require prior approval from a department director.
- All air travel must be booked through the company's designated travel agency, Horizon Travel, to ensure we receive our negotiated corporate rates.

Employees must submit expense reports within 14 days of returning from a business trip. Late submissions may result in delayed reimbursement. All reports must include original receipts or clear digital copies.

The full updated policy document is available on the company intranet under "Finance Policies." Please review it before your next business trip.
