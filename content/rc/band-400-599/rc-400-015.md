# Workplace Safety Reminder

To: All Staff — Building A and Building B
From: Facilities Management
Date: March 21, 2026
Subject: Updated Fire Safety Procedures

Following the recent fire safety inspection, we are implementing several updates to our emergency procedures. All employees should carefully review the changes outlined below.

New fire extinguishers have been installed on every floor in both buildings. The previous models were replaced because they had reached their five-year expiration date. Each floor now has two extinguishers located near the elevator lobby and the emergency stairwell. Employees are encouraged to familiarize themselves with the locations of these devices.

Emergency exit routes have been updated. Building A employees should now use the west stairwell as the primary exit route instead of the east stairwell, which will undergo structural maintenance from April through June. Building B exit routes remain unchanged.

A mandatory fire drill will be conducted on April 8 at 2:00 p.m. All employees must participate. During the drill, proceed calmly to the designated assembly point in the south parking lot. Floor wardens will verify attendance using department rosters.

Additionally, all department managers are required to attend a 90-minute fire safety briefing on April 3. Registration is available through the facilities management portal.

## Q1. Why were the old fire extinguishers replaced?
- A. They were damaged in a recent fire.
- B. They had reached their five-year expiration date.
- C. They did not meet new government regulations.
- D. They were the wrong type for the building.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states "the previous models were replaced because they had reached their five-year expiration date."

## Q2. Why should Building A employees avoid the east stairwell?
- A. It is being used for storage.
- B. It has been permanently closed.
- C. It will undergo structural maintenance.
- D. It is reserved for Building B employees.
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice explains the east stairwell "will undergo structural maintenance from April through June."

## Q3. What are department managers required to do?
- A. Purchase new safety equipment
- B. Conduct individual team drills
- C. Attend a 90-minute fire safety briefing
- D. Inspect all fire extinguishers personally
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice states "all department managers are required to attend a 90-minute fire safety briefing on April 3."
