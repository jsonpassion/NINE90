# Mandatory Cybersecurity Training

Notice to All Staff
From: IT Security Department
Date: March 20, 2026

All employees are required to complete the updated cybersecurity awareness training by April 30, 2026. This training is mandatory for every staff member, regardless of department or position level. The program has been redesigned to address recent increases in phishing attacks and data security threats affecting our industry.

The training consists of three online modules, each approximately 45 minutes long. Module 1 covers recognizing phishing emails and suspicious links. Module 2 focuses on password security and two-factor authentication. Module 3 addresses safe handling of confidential company data and client information.

Employees may complete the modules at their own pace through the company's learning management system. Each module ends with a short quiz, and a score of 80 percent or higher is required to pass. Those who do not pass may retake the quiz up to three times.

Managers are responsible for ensuring all team members complete the training on time. Completion records will be reviewed by the compliance department during the quarterly audit. Employees who have not finished by the deadline may face restricted access to company systems until the requirement is fulfilled.

## Q1. How many modules does the training program include?
- A. Two
- B. Three
- C. Four
- D. Five
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice states "the training consists of three online modules, each approximately 45 minutes long."

## Q2. What is the minimum passing score for each quiz?
- A. 70 percent
- B. 75 percent
- C. 80 percent
- D. 85 percent
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The notice specifies "a score of 80 percent or higher is required to pass."

## Q3. What consequence may employees face if they miss the deadline?
- A. A reduction in salary
- B. Restricted access to company systems
- C. Automatic termination
- D. A mandatory meeting with HR
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The notice warns that "employees who have not finished by the deadline may face restricted access to company systems until the requirement is fulfilled."
