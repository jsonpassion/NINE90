# IT System Migration

From: IT Department
To: All Employees
Date: March 24, 2026
Subject: Email System Migration — Action Required

Dear Employees,

We are migrating from our current email platform, MailConnect, to Microsoft Outlook beginning April 5. This migration will improve email reliability, increase storage capacity, and provide better integration with our other business tools.

The migration will take place in phases. Departments on the 1st and 2nd floors will be migrated on April 5. The 3rd and 4th floors will follow on April 12, and the 5th and 6th floors on April 19. During your department's migration day, email access may be unavailable for up to four hours, typically between 6:00 AM and 10:00 AM.

Before your migration date, please complete the following: First, delete unnecessary emails to reduce migration time. Second, save any important attachments to your OneDrive folder. Third, update your email signature using the template available on the IT portal. Finally, attend the 30-minute Outlook training session offered daily in the IT Training Room from March 28 to April 4.

All existing emails, contacts, and calendar entries will be transferred automatically. Your email address will remain the same. After migration, MailConnect will no longer be accessible.

For assistance, contact the IT Help Desk at extension 7000 or helpdesk@company.com.

## Q1. Why is the company changing email platforms?
- A. MailConnect is going out of business.
- B. To improve reliability, storage, and integration.
- C. Microsoft is offering a free trial.
- D. Employees requested the change.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email states the migration 'will improve email reliability, increase storage capacity, and provide better integration.'

## Q2. When will the 3rd floor be migrated?
- A. April 5
- B. April 12
- C. April 19
- D. April 26
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email says 'The 3rd and 4th floors will follow on April 12.'

## Q3. What will happen to employees' current email addresses?
- A. They will change to a new format.
- B. They will remain the same.
- C. They will need to be renewed.
- D. They will expire after 30 days.
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The email states 'Your email address will remain the same.'
