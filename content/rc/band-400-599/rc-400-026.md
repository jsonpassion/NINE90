# Product Launch Timeline

From: Michelle Torres, Product Manager
To: Cross-Functional Launch Team
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: EcoSmart Thermostat — Launch Timeline Update

Dear Team,

I wanted to provide an update on the EcoSmart Thermostat launch timeline. After discussions with engineering and quality assurance, we have decided to push the launch date from April 15 to May 6. This additional time will allow us to address the connectivity issues identified during beta testing and complete final regulatory certifications.

The revised schedule is as follows: Engineering will deliver the final firmware update by April 10. Quality assurance testing will run from April 11 to April 22. Marketing materials and the product landing page must be finalized by April 25. Pre-orders will open on April 28, with the official product launch and shipping beginning on May 6.

I understand this delay is disappointing, but launching with a reliable product is critical for our brand reputation. Our competitor analysis shows that similar products have faced negative reviews due to connectivity problems, and we want to avoid the same outcome.

Each team lead should update their respective project plans and confirm revised deadlines with me by March 28. Please flag any resource constraints immediately so we can address them proactively.

Thank you for your continued dedication to making this launch a success.

## Q1. Why was the launch date delayed?
- A. The marketing materials were not ready
- B. A competitor launched a similar product
- C. Connectivity issues and pending regulatory certifications
- D. The manufacturing facility was behind schedule
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The delay is to address connectivity issues from beta testing and complete regulatory certifications.

## Q2. When will pre-orders open?
