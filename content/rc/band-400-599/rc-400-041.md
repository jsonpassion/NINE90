# IT System Migration Plan

From: James Lee, Chief Technology Officer
To: All Department Managers
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: Planned Migration to New Enterprise Resource Planning System

Dear Managers,

I am writing to outline the timeline and expectations for our migration from the current SAP system to Oracle Cloud ERP, which has been approved by the board as part of our digital transformation strategy.

The migration will occur in three phases. Phase 1 (April 1 - May 15) will involve data mapping and cleaning, during which each department will need to designate two representatives to work with the IT team to verify the accuracy of their departmental data. Phase 2 (May 16 - June 30) will be the parallel testing period, where both old and new systems will run simultaneously. All transactions must be entered in both systems during this period. Phase 3 (July 1 - July 15) is the final cutover, after which the SAP system will be decommissioned.

Training will be provided in three waves corresponding to each phase. The first training session, covering basic navigation and data entry in the new system, begins April 14. Each session runs for a full day, and attendance is mandatory for at least one representative per department.

The total project budget is $1.8 million, and we have engaged Accenture as our implementation partner. A dedicated help desk for migration-related issues will be operational from April 1 through August 31, available Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM.

Your cooperation is essential to a smooth transition. Please confirm your department representatives by March 31.

## Q1. During which phase will both systems run at the same time?
- A. Phase 1
- B. Phase 2
- C. Phase 3
- D. All phases
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Phase 2 is the parallel testing period where both systems run simultaneously.

## Q2. How many department representatives are needed for data verification?
- A. One
- B. Two
- C. Three
- D. Four
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Each department will need to designate two representatives.

## Q3. When will the SAP system be decommissioned?
- A. After May 15
- B. After July 15
- C. After June 30
- D. After August 31
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Phase 3 ends July 15, after which the SAP system will be decommissioned.
