# Conference Registration

From: Events Coordination Team
To: All Sales Division Staff
Date: March 20, 2026
Subject: National Sales Conference — Early Registration

Dear Colleagues,

We are pleased to announce that registration is now open for the 12th Annual National Sales Conference, which will be held from May 18 to May 20 at the Grand Metropolitan Hotel in Chicago. This year's theme is "Building Client Relationships in a Digital World."

The conference will feature keynote speakers from leading technology and sales consulting firms, as well as breakout sessions on topics including social selling, customer retention strategies, and AI-powered sales tools. A networking dinner will be held on the evening of May 19.

The early registration fee is $450 per person, available until April 15. After that date, the standard fee of $575 applies. The company will cover the registration fee and travel expenses for up to two representatives per regional office. Additional attendees may register at their own expense with manager approval.

To register, please visit www.nationalsalesconf.com and use the company code NSC2026. Hotel rooms at the conference rate of $189 per night must be booked separately through the hotel's reservation line.

Please contact the Events Coordination Team at events@company.com if you need assistance.
