# Clinic Relocation Announcement

Dear Patients,

We are writing to inform you that Lakewood Family Clinic will be relocating to a new facility on August 1. Our new address is 310 Birch Avenue, Suite 200, just three blocks from our current location on Maple Street.

The decision to relocate was made to accommodate our growing patient base and to provide an improved healthcare experience. The new facility is nearly twice the size of our current office and will feature additional examination rooms, an on-site laboratory, and a dedicated pediatric wing.

During the transition week of July 28 to August 1, the clinic will have limited hours. Urgent appointments will be available, but routine checkups should be scheduled before July 28 or after August 5. Patients with existing appointments during the transition week will be contacted individually by our staff.

All patient records will be transferred securely to the new location. Your insurance information and account details will remain unchanged. If you have questions about the move, please call our office at 555-0192.
