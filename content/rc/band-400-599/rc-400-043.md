# New Client Onboarding Process

To: Account Management Team
From: Richard Torres, Director of Client Services
Date: March 21, 2026
Subject: Revised Client Onboarding Process

After reviewing client feedback and analyzing our first-year retention data, I have approved a revised onboarding process for all new clients effective April 1. The previous process averaged 21 days from contract signing to full service activation, and our goal is to reduce this to 14 days or less.

The revised process includes four key changes. First, a dedicated onboarding specialist will be assigned to each new client within 24 hours of contract signing, replacing the previous model where the sales representative handled onboarding. Second, a kickoff call must be scheduled within 3 business days of contract execution to establish expectations, introduce the service team, and confirm technical requirements. Third, all data migration and system setup must be completed within 10 business days, with daily progress updates sent to the client. Fourth, a satisfaction survey will be sent on day 14 and day 30 to capture early feedback.

The onboarding team will consist of four specialists, two of whom are currently being recruited. The remaining two will be existing team members who will receive specialized training during the week of March 31. Each specialist will handle no more than 5 concurrent onboardings to ensure quality.

Success will be measured by three metrics: time to activation, client satisfaction scores at day 30, and first-year retention rates. Quarterly reviews will assess the effectiveness of the new process.

Please schedule time with me individually this week to discuss how this affects your current accounts.

## Q1. What is the target onboarding timeline?
- A. 7 days
- B. 10 days
- C. 14 days
- D. 21 days
- Answer: C
- Explanation: The goal is to reduce onboarding to 14 days or less, down from the previous 21 days.

## Q2. How many concurrent onboardings can each specialist handle?
- A. 3
- B. 5
- C. 7
- D. 10
- Answer: B
- Explanation: Each specialist will handle no more than 5 concurrent onboardings.

## Q3. When must the kickoff call be scheduled?
- A. Within 24 hours of signing
- B. Within 5 business days of signing
- C. Within 3 business days of signing
- D. Within 10 business days of signing
- Answer: C
- Explanation: A kickoff call must be scheduled within 3 business days of contract execution.
