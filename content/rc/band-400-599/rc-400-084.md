# Customer Loyalty Program Launch

Dear Valued Customer,

We are pleased to introduce the Hartfield's Rewards Card, our new customer loyalty program launching on June 1. As a frequent shopper at Hartfield's Department Store, you are among the first to be invited to join.

As a Rewards Card member, you will earn one point for every dollar spent in-store or online. When you reach 500 points, you will receive a $25 gift certificate that can be used on any future purchase. Members also enjoy exclusive benefits such as early access to seasonal sales, free gift wrapping, and a 15 percent birthday discount.

Signing up is easy and free. Simply visit the customer service counter at any Hartfield's location or register online at www.hartfields.com/rewards. You will receive your card immediately at the store or within seven business days by mail for online registrations.

We value your continued support and look forward to rewarding your loyalty. If you have any questions, please call our rewards hotline at 555-0277.

Sincerely,
Margaret Wells
Customer Relations Manager

## Q1. How many points are needed for a $25 gift certificate?
- A. 100 points
- B. 250 points
- C. 1,000 points
- D. 500 points
- Answer: D
- Explanation: The letter says "When you reach 500 points, you will receive a $25 gift certificate."

## Q2. Which benefit is NOT mentioned for Rewards Card members?
- A. Early access to sales
- B. Free gift wrapping
- C. Free shipping on online orders
- D. A birthday discount
- Answer: C
- Explanation: Free shipping is not mentioned. The benefits listed are early access to sales, free gift wrapping, and a 15 percent birthday discount.

## Q3. How can customers sign up for the program?
- A. By calling the rewards hotline only
- B. At any store location or online
- C. By sending an email to customer service
- D. Only at the main branch
- Answer: B
- Explanation: The letter says to "visit the customer service counter at any Hartfield's location or register online."
