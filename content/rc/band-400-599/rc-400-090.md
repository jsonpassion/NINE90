Your registration includes access to all keynote presentations, panel discussions, and the networking reception on October 19. Workshop sessions require separate registration and have limited availability. We recommend signing up early on the event website.

The early-bird registration rate of $450 has been applied to your account. The standard rate is $600. Please note that the conference hotel, the Grandview Inn, is offering a discounted room rate of $145 per night for conference attendees. To receive this rate, reservations must be made by September 30 using the code MKTSUM26.

A detailed event program will be emailed to all registered participants by October 1. If you have any questions in the meantime, please reply to this email or call our registration office at 555-0815.

We look forward to seeing you in October!
