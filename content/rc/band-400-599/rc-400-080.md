# Annual Performance Review

From: Human Resources Director
To: All People Managers
Date: March 24, 2026
Subject: Annual Performance Review Process — FY2025

Dear Managers,

The annual performance review cycle for fiscal year 2025 begins on April 1. This email outlines the timeline, process changes, and your responsibilities as a people manager.

The review period covers April 1, 2025, through March 31, 2026. Self-assessments will be open in the PerformPlus system from April 1 to April 14. Managers should complete their evaluations between April 15 and April 30. One-on-one review meetings with each direct report must be conducted between May 1 and May 16. Final ratings must be submitted in PerformPlus by May 23.

This year, we are introducing a new competency framework that replaces the previous five-point rating scale with a four-level model: Needs Development, Meets Expectations, Exceeds Expectations, and Outstanding. This change is designed to encourage more meaningful conversations about growth and reduce rating inflation.

Managers should base their evaluations on documented accomplishments, feedback from peers and stakeholders, and alignment with department goals. At least two specific examples should support each competency rating.

Calibration sessions will be held by division from May 5 to May 9 to ensure consistency across teams. HR business partners will facilitate these sessions.

A training webinar on the new competency framework will be offered on March 28 at 11:00 AM. Registration is available on the HR portal.

Thank you for your commitment to developing our people.
