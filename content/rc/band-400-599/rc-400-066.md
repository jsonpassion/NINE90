# Customer Service Update

From: Operations Director
To: Customer Service Team
Date: March 22, 2026
Subject: New Return Policy — Effective April 1

Dear Team,

Starting April 1, we are updating our return policy to improve customer satisfaction while reducing processing costs. Please familiarize yourselves with the following changes before the new policy takes effect.

The return window for most products will be extended from 30 days to 45 days from the date of purchase. Electronics will remain at the current 15-day return window due to rapid depreciation. All returns must be accompanied by a receipt or proof of purchase, which can now include a digital receipt from our app or website.

Refunds for items returned within the first 14 days will be processed to the original payment method. Items returned after 14 days but within the return window will be refunded as store credit only. Shipping costs for online returns will be covered by the company for orders over $50. For orders under $50, customers are responsible for return shipping.

A new FAQ page is being added to our website to help customers understand the updated policy. Please refer customers to www.ourstore.com/returns for detailed information. Training materials will be available on the staff portal by March 28.

Thank you for your cooperation in making this transition smooth for our customers.
