# Customer Support Manager Position

NOW HIRING: CUSTOMER SUPPORT MANAGER
Atlas Financial Services — Chicago, IL

Atlas Financial Services is looking for an experienced Customer Support Manager to oversee our growing support team of 25 representatives. This position reports directly to the Vice President of Client Relations and plays a critical role in maintaining our industry-leading customer satisfaction scores.

Key responsibilities include managing daily operations of the support center, developing and implementing training programs for new and existing staff, analyzing customer feedback data to identify service improvements, creating monthly performance reports for senior management, and handling escalated customer issues that require management intervention.

Qualifications: Bachelor's degree in business administration or a related field, minimum 7 years of customer service experience with at least 3 years in a supervisory role, proficiency in CRM software (Salesforce experience preferred), excellent written and verbal communication skills, and the ability to work flexible hours including occasional weekends.

Compensation includes a base salary of $78,000 to $95,000, performance bonuses up to 15 percent of base salary, full medical and dental coverage, a 401(k) plan with 6 percent company match, and tuition reimbursement for approved professional development courses.

Interested candidates should submit their resume and a brief management philosophy statement to hr@atlasfinancial.com. Applications will be accepted until April 21, 2026.
