# Social Media Policy for Employees

COMPANY SOCIAL MEDIA POLICY

To: All Employees
From: Communications Department
Date: March 20, 2026
Subject: Employee Social Media Guidelines

As social media continues to play an important role in both personal and professional communication, Evergreen Industries has established the following guidelines to protect both our employees and the company's reputation.

Employees are free to use personal social media accounts on their own time. However, when your affiliation with Evergreen Industries is apparent or disclosed, the following rules apply: You must clearly state that your views are your own and do not represent the company. You may not share confidential business information, including financial data, unreleased product details, or internal strategy documents. You should not make disparaging comments about the company, colleagues, clients, or competitors.

Employees who manage or contribute to official company social media accounts must follow the Social Media Content Calendar approved by the Communications Department. All posts representing the company must be reviewed by a Communications team member before publication. Response times for customer inquiries received through social media should not exceed 4 hours during business hours.

Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action ranging from a verbal warning for minor infractions to termination for serious breaches such as sharing confidential information. If you are unsure whether a post complies with this policy, please consult the Communications Department at social@evergreen.com before publishing.

This policy is subject to review every 12 months. The next scheduled review is March 2027.

## Q1. What must employees state when their company affiliation is known?
- A. That their views are their own
- B. Their job title and department
- C. That they have approval to post
- D. Their employee ID number
- Answer: A
- Explanation: Employees must clearly state that their views are their own and do not represent the company.
