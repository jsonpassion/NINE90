# Flexible Work Schedule Pilot Program

To: All Employees
From: Laura Bennett, Chief Operating Officer
Date: May 5

I am pleased to announce that starting June 1, the company will launch a six-month pilot program for flexible work schedules. Under this program, eligible employees may choose from two options: a compressed workweek of four 10-hour days or a staggered schedule with adjusted start and end times.

Employees who select the compressed workweek will work Monday through Thursday and have Fridays off. Those who prefer the staggered schedule may start their workday as early as 7:00 a.m. or as late as 10:00 a.m., as long as they complete eight hours of work each day.

To be eligible, employees must have been with the company for at least one year and have received a satisfactory or above rating on their most recent performance review. Participation requires manager approval, and certain roles that require fixed schedules may not qualify.

Interested employees should submit an application through the HR portal by May 25. The program will be evaluated in December, and if successful, it may become a permanent policy.
